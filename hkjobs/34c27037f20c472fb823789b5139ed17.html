Agency Securities Finance Operations provides regional operational support for our Agency Securities Finance business.

ASF as an agent lender provides equity and fixed income securities to broker dealers to facilitate their proprietary and client trading strategies.  Where they have a requirement to borrow securities the lending desk negotiates a fee for the loan based on supply and demand and the type of underlying strategy.  With 5 desks globally, JPMC offers a 24 hour service in 37 different markets.

Based in Hong Kong, the successful candidate will report to the APAC Agency Securities Finance (ASF) Operations lead and be responsible for the following:

  • Local coverage and governance of the ASF HK Operations team
  • Adherence to all critical business and client SLA’s, regulatory requirements, and adherence to exchange protocols across Asia-ex ANZ & Japan (6 markets)
  • Drive resolution and management of inquiries, leveraging internal teams globally and regionally
  • Monitoring and resolution of risk indicators to ensure that senior management are aware of any issues that may have an adverse financial or reputational impact
  • ASF infrastructure development, both tactical and strategic across the delivery horizons, lobbying for automation and other improvements to minimize risk and improve efficiency
  • Relationship management of key Business, Product, Technology, Operations and other Senior Stakeholders in Compliance, Audit and Business Control

The role will provide the successful applicant the platform to deliver Operational excellence in core domains across People Leadership, Digital and Data Transformation, Infrastructure Strategy and Deployment, Culture and Conduct Leadership and Controls.

Requirements:

  • Minimum of 15 years of relevant industry experience, ideally with a range of core Operations processing expertise in Securities Finance
  • Strong leadership skills
  • Superior stakeholder management skills – a proven ability to connect with, build relationships and quickly win the trust of business leaders
  • Exposure to the Asia Pacific regulatory environment construct and nuances, rules and exchange protocols
  • Experience of digital tools and data management
