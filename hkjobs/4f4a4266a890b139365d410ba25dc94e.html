Job Description


Brand: Citadel


Role: Physical Natural Gas Specialists – North America


Established in 2014, Citadel Energy Marketing has one of the largest portfolios of storage and transport assets in the West; with a growing footprint in Canada, the Midcontinent and Northeast. We are responsible for connecting natural gas producers with utilities and other market participants across North America. We partner with our clients to manage risk, finance operations and gain strategic insight into their businesses and the markets within which they operate. 


Our success rests on the individuals we employ across a range of roles including origination, trading, analytics, scheduling and optimization. This experience and asset base allows us to offer competitive and flexible services that are tailored to our clients’ needs. A career at Citadel offers you the chance to generate and implement your own innovative solutions to really make a difference.



Location: Greenwich, CT, Houston


Your Opportunity



  • Play a key role in physical gas trading, analysis, scheduling, origination, and structuring

  • Maintain a deep knowledge of the physical U.S. gas markets, pipelines, regulations, and constraints

  • Challenge consensus views within the physical space to outperform the market

  • Identify opportunities within your specialism that create alpha outside of the platform’s current capabilities

  • Work closely with internal stakeholders such as traders, analysts, schedulers, originators, structurers, marketers and members of the middle office team to optimize and enhance the team’s approach to the physical markets



Your Skills and Talents



  • Prior U.S. physical gas experience

  • Excellent analytical and quantitative skills, with strong attention to detail

  • Strong written and verbal communications skills

  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and thrive in a fast-paced team environment



Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience in statistics, mathematics, computer science, finance, or economics
