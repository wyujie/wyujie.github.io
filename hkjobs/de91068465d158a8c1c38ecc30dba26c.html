UBS - Wealth Management Front Support Office Senior Due Diligence Analyst


2023-02-08 - Operations - Hong Kong

Your role

Are you incredibly organized with an eye for detail? Are you passionate about delivering best in class Client Experience? Do you enjoy identifying the most effective and efficient approaches for Wealth Management business? Are you good at constructively challenging business stakeholders around you?

We’re looking for someone like that to:

• perform AML controls on names screening during client onboarding and periodic review
• review results and eliminate false hits using established guidelines and policies
• liaise with internal stakeholders to resolve open hits, and escalate adverse news and risks appropriately
• prepare accurate and complete internal documentation based on search results
• participate in ad-hoc projects, including process improvement initiatives and enhancement of existing controls
• proactively identify gaps in current process against global due diligence policies

Your team

You’ll be working in the Wealth Management Front Support Office team in Hong Kong. We provide support on private banking clients in line with the regulatory requirements and UBS group policies. The scope of work includes liaising with client advisors, business, and internal stakeholders to ensure that an excellent and outstanding client experience is achieved. We support the Hong Kong Wealth Management business for Onboarding and periodic KYC services, and you will act as a business enabler to help UBS generate revenue.

Your expertise

You have:
• at least 5 years of working experience
• 2 years of experience in managing teams
• experience in Wealth Management Onboarding, Maintenance, or Periodic KYC Review in Hong Kong and/or Singapore regulatory landscape is preferred but not essential
• experience in leading and/or implementing process changes & automation favored
• client focused and motivated to work in partnership with front to back stakeholders to define practical solutions that meets the internal policies and regulatory requirements
• strong risk and control mindset with a structured and logical approach to analysis and problem-solving

You are:
• a great coach, keen on developing talent and building strong and happy teams
• attention to details and diligent
• good at prioritization and can handle large volumes of information whilst meeting tight deadlines
• an effective team player, with professional and positive attitude
• adaptable to a dynamic and changing environment
• good at improving processes and enhancing workflows
