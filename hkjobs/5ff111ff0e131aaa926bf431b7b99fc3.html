Position Overview

Details of the Division and Team:

ICG is a global sales team covering various institutional clients including Hedge Funds, Insurance companies, Asset Managers, Banks, Central Banks, Sovereign Wealth Funds and Private Banks. In APAC, we have salespeople located across the regional hubs in Singapore, Hong Kong, Tokyo, Sydney as well as in several countries across Asia.

  • Competitive Salary and non-contributory pension

  • Life Assurance and Private Healthcare for you and your family

Your key responsibilities:

  • Work in partnership with voice sales team on client relationship management of key FX accounts to achieve revenue and market share goals.

  • Strengthen and build relationships with coverage client base.

  • Detailed understanding of client’s execution model and electronic workflow to provide suitable and appropriate platform solutions.

  • Prospecting for new client electronic business within coverage base, sell and present DB supported platforms to meet clients requirements (including origination and onboarding as per DB policies)

  • Leverage knowledge of Deutsche Bank’s eCommerce product and trading relationships to provide clients with bespoke workflow solution to meet their needs in order to gain wallet share and relevance.

  • Work closely with the trading team across FX Spot, FX Forward, FX Options, FX Segregated Execution, Emerging & Developed market rates on optimization and analysis of client flows.

  • Share and collaborate with team and trading on product development ideas to enhance Deutsche Bank’s electronic platform.

  • Perform regular client reviews and visits with pro-active follow up

  • Represent Deutsche Bank at conferences and industry presentations

  • Lead electronic macro sales coverage for North Asia (Hong Kong, Taiwan, Korea China) across all institutional clients

  • Back up sales coverage for rest of Asia ex Japan Australia (11 countries)

Your skills and experience:

  • Voice and electronic FIC Institutional sales experience in Tier 1 Investment Bank with 7+ years in FX Electronic Sales preferred.

  • Work experience in at least 2 of the core regional markets (these being China, Hong Kong and Singapore – ability to cover and back up entire region)

  • Investment bank electronic macro platform sales specialist knowledge required.

  • Mandarin language skill (written and spoken) is required due to client coverage of China and Taiwan where clients communicate exclusively in Mandarin including documentation and communication with infrastructure partners

  • Prior experience in both client facing and product management roles

  • Able to up sell workflow (third party payments, workflow solutions, segregated execution, cross sell related products like prime brokerage)

  • Detailed project manager – able to handle migration projects and coordinate with operations/legal/trading effectively

Role is required to be performed primarily on-site at the International Commerce Centre. Relevant health and safety requirements apply.
