Responsibilities include:

  • Execution & microstructure analysis – run independent analysis to drive performance optimisation and product enhancement.
  • Execution advisory – collaborate  with cash equities desks and external clients to optimise and improve execution performance using transaction cost analysis.
  • Product development – working closely with several teams across the bank (technology, quant research, business management, compliance, control functions) to deliver a high quality product to commercial effect.
  • Product management – ensure the product remains compliant with latest market developments, rules and regulations and has appropriate trading controls and capacity.
  • Product governance – communicate to stakeholders on project opportunities, progress & risks. Provide regular updates on business metrics and contribute updates to E-trading control and Best Execution forums.

 

Qualifications

  • Ideal candidate will have at least 4-5 years of relevant experience in Equities, including Electronic Trading.
  • Demonstrable knowledge about common execution algorithms (VWAP, IS, CLOSE, etc.) and Smart Order Routing strategies.
  • Strong analytical skills, able to work with large datasets and ability to present findings in a clear and concise manner. Ideally experience with KDB or equivalents.
  • Experience in performing pre and post trade transaction cost analysis.
  • Good technical and quantitative skills – ability to articulate requirements and contribute to discussion with Technology and Quantitative Research teams.
  • Knowledge of Asia Equities market structure.
  • Execution oriented with understanding of trade lifecycle.
  • Understand the different types of risk and can discuss approaches to managing these risks.
  • Entrepreneurial drive, demonstrating creativity in providing solutions to problems, and looking for alternative approaches versus the norm.
  • Ability to interact comfortably with business, clients and management and develop and maintain relationships with internal and external partners.
  • Ability to communicate concepts and ideas, both verbally and via documentation, and be able to defend their validity and target messages to different audiences.
  • Calm under pressure and ability to thrive on the challenge of competitive pressures and remain focused on client needs.
  • Open and honest communications. Escalates issues, risks and concerns promptly.
  • Organised and structured approach to managing work load.
  • Self-directed, highly motivated, and able to work independently.
