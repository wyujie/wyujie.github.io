A Career with Point72’s Academy

Are you a student interested in a career in investing, and graduating in Fall 2024 or Spring 2025? If so, we invite you to apply to our 8-week summer internship that runs from June through August 2024.


Point72 Academy Summer Internship

The Point72 Academy is an investment analyst training program that will equip you to define your career path in an ever-changing industry, and the internship is the first step in that journey. We teach you the fundamentals of finance, research, and insights into the public markets.

 

You will learn what it’s like to be a research analyst through classroom instruction led by former investment professionals, portfolio casework that will support a real investment team, and time with your dedicated mentor.


We’re training the next generation of investors at Point72.

Point72 is a global asset management firm that prioritizes developing the careers of our portfolio managers (PMs) and analysts. We value independent thinkers, which is why we started the Point72 Academy to train the next generation of talent. We’re a firm that thrives on ideas, and we’ve found that the Point72 Academy has provided us with diverse viewpoints and perspectives, and we are able to offer support in their growth and development as towards becoming analysts and portfolio managers.


The annual base salary range is $130000.0-$140000.0 (USD) . Actual compensation offered to candidate may vary from posted hiring range based upon geographic location, work experience, education, and/or skill level among other things. Details about eligibility for bonus compensation (if applicable) will be finalized at the time of offer.

Career Progression

The majority of our Summer Internship participants to date have received offers to join the full-time, 11-month Academy Associate Program. US internship and all full-time program participants work in our New York City offices during classroom training, but Point72 has locations around the world and offers placements in multiple cities and countries. You will have the opportunity to select and rank multiple offices in your online application should you be interested in more than one. 


What you’ll do:

  • Learn the basic knowledge and skills you’ll need to add value as an analyst alongside your internship peers.
  • Train on accounting, modeling, and presenting skills, in addition to our compliance program.
  • Take those skills and sit on a desk with an investment team to experience the day-to-day life of an equities analyst and learn about one of the several sectors our Portfolio Managers cover.
  • Working with Academy coaches, you will round out the internship diving deeper into data analysis and prepare a final pitch on an investment idea. 



What to expect

Your application and resume are a way for us to start getting to know you. We will begin reviewing your profile once your online application is complete. If progressed to the next stage, you will be invited to complete a questionnaire.

If you are selected to continue the process, you will receive an email invitation to complete a case study to showcase your experiences and how you think about businesses.

There is not a set application deadline for this position—we strongly encourage you to apply and complete all the requirements as soon as you feel ready to begin the hiring process.
