Role Description

The Fund Services Operation Services Delivery (OSD)/Client Reporting Team deliver a range of services to institutional investors based around the Asian region. The team takes direct responsibility for the production and delivery of client reporting and related accounting/portfolio information. The OSD team are responsible for the day-to-day service management of clients; ensuring queries are answered, problems are resolved and issues are escalated. As the point of escalation for the clients, a broad knowledge of fund accounting is required together with sound experience of managing clients.

Responsibilities:

  • Client Support and Engagement
    • Represent J.P. Morgan as operational contact for client(s) across fund services products
    • Own the end-to-end service delivery, including tracking of quality/timeliness of client deliverables, resolution of client queries, providing service review and client training, and maintaining service level document (SLD) with client(s) on regular basis
    • Manage client change requests in partnership with client, client services, product, product development through the life-cycle of the request
    • Act as point-of-contact to represent fund services operation in support of new/incremental business onboarding
    • Manage/resolve delivery issues and ensure timely communication to client(s) in conjunction with client services teams
    • Build effective working relationship with client(s) and relevant external parties (e.g. Trustee, 3rd party Fund Manager, Auditor) to support seamless interactions and escalation
    • Work with client(s) on continuous service and operational improvements, and provide thought leadership in view of market and industry developments
  • Production Management
    • Own the production, validation and delivery of client reports in accordance to SLD
    • Perform analysis of exceptions related to client reporting production, and escalate any potential issue for timely resolution
    • Conduct on-going review to identify and implement process improvements to achieve higher degree of quality, efficiency, and risk management controls
    • Provide appropriate escalation of all issues, service updates and progress updates for the client to Management and internal stakeholders

Requirements:

  • University graduate with major in Finance/Accounting/ Computer Science/ Mathematics preferred
  • 8+ years of experience in financial institution especially Fund Services; Hands on experience in Private Equity and Offshore Fund Administration services experience is a strong advantage
  • 5+ years of experience in client facing and team management roles preferred
  • Fluency in written & spoken English; Chinese, Japanese, Korean language skill both written and spoken skill is a strong  advantage
  • Highly motivated, end to end ownership, execution driven and able to drive decision making
  • Strong interpersonal skills, ability to influence and negotiate for results
