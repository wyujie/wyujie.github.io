Millennium - Quantitative Analyst – Equity Derivatives


2023-01-15 - Technology - Hong Kong

Quantitative Analyst – Equity Derivatives

We are assembling a strong Quant Technology team to build our next generation in-house analytics and trader support tools. This team develops and maintains the in-house pricing libraries to support trading in Fixed Income, Equity Derivatives, Credit, Commodities and FX business at Millennium. Our environment is dynamic and fast-paced environment with excellent growth opportunities.

Responsibilities:

  • Work closely with Quants globally to develop pricing, pre-trade analysis tools and risk analytics for our in-house pricing library, focusing on equity derivatives
  • Work closely with Equity IT (in particular in Asia) for integration of models into risk systems – help using models, explain numbers, build new package, investigate technical issues
  • Interact with Portfolio Managers to gather requirements, discuss modelling approaches and methodologies for valuation and risk
  • Work with Middle Office and Risk Management teams in Asia – explain P&L and Risk, valuation methodology, etc.

Mandatory Requirements:

  • Familiarity with Equity derivatives products, including variance derivatives, VIX, barrier options and exotic products
  • Good knowledge of Object-Oriented Programming, in particular C++
  • Strong analytical and mathematical skills
  • Good problem-solving capabilities
  • Solid communication and inter-personal skills
  • Able to work independently in a fast-paced environment.
  • Detail oriented, organized, demonstrating thoroughness and strong ownership of work

Preferred Requirements:

  • Knowledge of Python or Java
  • Familiarity with Linux
  • Experience working with other asset classes (Fixed-income, FX, etc)
