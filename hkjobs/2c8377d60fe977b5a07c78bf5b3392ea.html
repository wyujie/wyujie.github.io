Global Custody Asset Servicing, Securities Services

The Asset Servicing team is an operational team processing high risk and complex voluntary corporate actions for Global Custody Markets within Securities Services.

Your primary role will be to handle large scale transactions in a fast paced working environment such as reviewing daily market announcements to extract key information relevant to our clients, process timely daily Corporate Actions instructions to ensure we meet market deadlines and accurately process cash and stock payments to our clients.

You will also participate in key operational project and digital tools initiatives aiming for continuous improvements to decrease manual touch points and increase the straight through processing rate of our current processes to achieve operational excellence and efficiency.

The team operates in partnership with various stakeholders to ensure that service level agreements are met to deliver high class service to our institutional clients. Internally, as an operational team, you will communicate and handle queries from various global middle office team to ensure we offer timely and efficient level of issues resolution as well as collaborating with regional processing team on operational matters including monitoring, analyzing daily corporate actions volume and advocating continuous improvement of our current processes by leveraging various systems and digital tools across the organization. Externally, you will foster relationship with custodian banks across Global markets to ensure Corporate Actions are processed efficiently in a timely and accurate manner.

Our mission is to provide a world-class service experience and achieve quality leadership in the industry through measurable and proven service performance.

As part of the team, your key Accountabilities/Responsibilities are:

  • Manage daily operational processing on Corporate Actions for Global Markets (event announcement, instruction management, payment processing)
  • Act as a single point of contact for internal and external stakeholders to ensure timely issue resolution on corporate actions queries
  • Collaborate with various teams within securities services across locations to ensure corporate actions are processed within a timely and accurately manner
  • Ensure internal procedures are followed with strong risk and control awareness
  • Take part in key projects and initiatives to achieve efficiency and operational excellence
  • Lead and drive change management related to CA processes and system enhancement
