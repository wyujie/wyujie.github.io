A Career with Long/Short Equities at Point72 - Quant Analyst

Long/short equity is Point72’s core strategy and its success is dependent upon our sector-based investing teams. Using fundamental research, our Research Analysts inform the investment strategies of our Portfolio Managers. Through our Point72 University, you have access to an unparalleled training and coaching curriculum – so you can create your best chance of success. We offer you a clear path based on your abilities, hard work, and performance. Join us for a career at the forefront of investing.


What you’ll do

  • Role:
  • We’re looking for an exceptional fresh graduate or entry level candidate to work as a quant analyst within a fundamental long/short investment team. The analyst will develop his/her career in systematic investing, with specialization of quantamental investing.


  • Responsibilities:
  • Work in mixed quant and fundamental environment.
  • Support the fundamental portfolio manager on data analysis. 
  • Conduct quantitative research into market data, develop alpha models and systematic investment strategies, and generate data-driven trade ideas.

What’s required

  • Requirements:
  • M.S. or PhD in science, engineering, economics, finance, or other quantitative disciplines
  • Fresh graduate or <3 years working experiences
  • Strong analytical skills, attention to details
  • Solid programming skills in Python
  • Advanced knowledge in machine learning
  • Team player, willing to develop career in collaborative environment
  • Passion in both systematic and fundamental investing
