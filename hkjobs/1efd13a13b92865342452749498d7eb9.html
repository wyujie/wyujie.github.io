J.P. Morgan - Fund Operations - Associate


2023-02-14 - Fund Servicing - Hong Kong

Being part of the Fund Reporting team, you will have the opportunity to liaise with internal and external stakeholders including auditors, trustees, service providers, middle office, product administration, product development, legal, operation controls and risk.

This is a unique opportunity to gain an in depth knowledge of asset management and contribute to a growing international business in a fast paced and innovative environment. 

Job responsibilities

  • Coordinate audit engagements with auditors, trustees, service provider and relevant internal stakeholders to ensure that the semi-annual and annual reports for pooled funds comply with regulatory requirements and deadlines
  • Review QFII/RQFII Funds’ financial statements
  • Perform oversight and coordination on regulatory and internal reporting, distributions and fund fees
  • Coordinate with various internal parties to ensure compliance in all operational areas including on-boarding of new clients and funds as well as resolving complex issues
  • Ensure operational processes and controls are in compliance to corporate policies and regulatory requirements 
  • To drive global efficiencies and business performance by optimizing, managing and maintaining operational processes
  • Participate in ad-hoc projects and process improvements

Required qualifications, capabilities, and skills

  • Degree holder in Accounting or Finance
  • Minimum 3 years of working experience in audit or financial industry, ideally with some fund accounting experience
  • An exceptional team player with a high level of integrity and self-discipline
  • Detail minded with good problem solving skill
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills with excellent command of written and spoken English, Chinese and Cantonese

 Preferred qualifications, capabilities, and skills

  • Professional qualification or big 4 audit experience is preferred
  • Willing to accept challenges, changes and embrace cross-functional rotation
  • CPA is preferred
