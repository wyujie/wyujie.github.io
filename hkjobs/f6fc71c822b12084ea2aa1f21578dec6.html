Job Description


Investment Associates work closely with Investment Analysts and Portfolio Managers to research investment ideas, utilizing a fundamental bottoms-up approach to stock selection in a market neutral context. Successful Investment Associates are given greater responsibility, with the ultimate goal of growing into an Investment Analyst Role.  


Key Responsibilities:


  • Conduct differentiated, bottoms-up fundamental analysis of companies, business models, relevant industry metrics and industries, becoming a go-to expert on a sub-sector of companies

  • Perform detailed valuation, and construct in-depth company financial models in Excel

  • Synthesize, communicate, and present long and short investment ideas to an Investment Analyst and/or Portfolio Manager

  • Actively participate in quarterly earnings calls and monitor material news events related to companies under coverage

  • Attend industry conferences and management meetings (includes travel)

  • Identify and analyze relevant industry metrics

  • Evaluate new coverage opportunities



Skillset Requirements:


  • Prior investment banking, private equity, long-only and/or sell-side research experience

  • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience, preferably in finance, economics, engineering and/or computer science

  • Passion for fundamental analysis and public markets investing

  • Advanced understanding of accounting/financial statements

  • Valuation experience and excellent Excel modeling skills

  • Excellent analytical and quantitative skills, with strong attention to detail

  • Strong written and oral communication skills 



Opportunities available in New York, Chicago, San Francisco, Greenwich, Boston, Dallas, Miami


In accordance with New York City’s Pay Transparency Law, the base salary range for this role is $125,000 to $150,000. Base salary does not include other forms of compensation or benefits.
