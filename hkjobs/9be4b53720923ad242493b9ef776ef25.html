Your role

Are you able to perform fundamental credit analysis and assess complex transactions? Do you possess strong teamwork skills?

We’re looking for a Junior Credit Officer who is able to:
• Assess the credit standing of our loan and derivative counterparties from a fundamental perspective.
• Understand financial markets and the risks involved in financial instruments (Equities, Fixed Income securities, including Derivatives).
• Demonstrate leadership and people management skills.
• Build strong business partnerships with different internal functions.
• Communicate effectively. The role requires strong verbal and written communication skills.
• Work in a fast-paced environment and manage deadlines appropriately.

Your team

You’ll be joining Wealth Management Risk Control at UBS, the #1 Global Wealth Manager, and market leader in Asia.

You will be working in Hong Kong as part of the Dedicated Credit Risk Control team. We assess large, complex lending and derivative structures for sophisticated clients across North Asia.

As a Junior Credit Officer, you will play a critical role analyzing these risks and guiding more junior team members in the assessment of structured transactions. Also, you will work closely with senior stakeholders in Risk Control, as well as the Business.

Your expertise

• 2 to 3 years professional experience in Wealth Management Risk Control, Investment Banking Risk Control or Corporate Finance.
• Prior working experience as a Credit Officer or similar is preferred
• Positive working attitude
• Proactive and exemplifies willingness to learn
• Leadership and people management skills
• Fair understanding of financial statements (Balance Sheet, Income Statement, Cash Flow analysis)
• Strong analytical skills. Feels comfortable analyzing complex structures, and understands the risks involved in collateralized lending transactions
• University degree or equivalent
