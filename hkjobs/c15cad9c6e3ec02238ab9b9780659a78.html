Position Overview

Details of the Division and Team:

This role supports the Equity Financing APAC, Wealth Management APAC, ECM and all which holds OTC equity derivatives product on Equities platform.

What we will offer you:

A healthy, engaged and well-supported workforce is better equipped to do their best work and, more importantly, enjoy their lives inside and outside the workplace. That’s why we are committed to providing an environment with your development and wellbeing at its center.

You can expect:

  • Competitive Salary and non-contributory pension                                       

  • 20 days annual leave plus generous number of public holidays

  • Life Assurance and Private Healthcare for you and your family                  

  • A range of flexible benefits including retail discounts, gym benefits, wellbeing incentives                                                                          

  • The opportunity to support a wide ranging CSR program plus take volunteering leave days

Your key responsibilities:

  • Able to manage and faceoff with Front office on day-to-day queries including booking and control, risk P&L, regulatory, audit/system queries.

  • A SME to support Equity Financing and Structuring desk by performing daily BAU booking in various systems, rec and control, ensuring good front-to-back process from booking to settlement to represent correct risk and compliant to regulatory report/compliance/audit requirement, and facilitating new client account opening.

  • To involve in F2B transformation in process/control/system integration into FICC.

  • To support Wealth Management business’s equity derivatives products in Equities infrastructure including daily BAU, reconciliation investigation and resolution, adhoc queries relating to Risk&PnL  and regulatory reporting issues.

  • Engaging with internal stakeholders in various book and/or system& process migration onto new FIC platform from Equities platform.

  • To involve in NTA review and discussion on operations feasibility/readiness assessment to ensure no-fail and compliant to all relevant governance and policy.

Your skills and experience:

  • Minimum of 5-7 years of Middle Office (Trade support) experience on Equity,  preferably on Equity Derivatives/Corporate Derivatives.

  • Excellent market/product knowledge on Equity, front to back operations process & control and operations related audit and regulatory requirement.

  • A good team player with strong interpersonal and problem solving skills, able to work under pressure and to challenging deadlines.

  • Good communication skills with experience dealing with Front Office and other internal stakeholders.

  • Good knowledge/experience on FIC products (Credit/Rates) is a plus.


Role is required to be performed primarily on-site at the International Commerce Centre. Relevant health and safety requirements apply.
