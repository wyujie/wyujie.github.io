Overview:

DWS Group (DWS) is one of the world's leading asset managers with EUR 859bn of assets under management (as of 30 June 2021). Building on more than 60 years of experience, it has a reputation for excellence in Europe, Americas and APAC. DWS is recognised by clients globally as a trusted source for integrated investment solutions, stability and innovation across a full spectrum of investment disciplines.

The DWS Illiquid business is a diversified set of business activities including Private Equity, Private Real Estate, Private Infrastructure, Liquid Real Assets (Real Estate/Infrastructure and Commodity Securities), Sustainable Investments and Hedge Funds. The global team invests on behalf of a global client base that includes governments, corporations, individuals and institutional investors. Reporting to the Regional Head of AFC & Compliance APAC, you will also be part of the broader APAC-wide AM Compliance team, assist with providing Compliance support to the Illiquid & Trading business in Hong Kong.

Your key responsibilities:

This role will support both HK and Mainland China in relation to alternative/illiquid businesses.

  • Provide compliance advice with regard to the applicable regulations of asset management business, internal policies and procedures and general issues relating to a broad range of compliance-related issues
  • Advise business in relation to the setup and maintenance of related controls to ensure compliance of various requirements.
  • Review and monitor the effectiveness of level one controls and governance framework of the illiquid business
  • Monitor regulatory developments applicable to the business and, working with relevant departments, ensure that appropriate impact assessment, gap analysis and implementation steps are carried out in a timely manner
  • Delivering materials and training on regulatory matters or developments in policies and procedures including induction training for new joiners and one to one training initiatives
  • Provide compliance advice on development of products linked to illiquid investment strategies including appropriateness, reputational risk and distribution considerations
  • Advising on governance and conduct related topics in respect of closed- and open-ended funds of the illiquid business
  • Handle ad hoc enquires from the illiquid business
  • Perform periodic compliance risk assessments on DWS business activities
  • Participate in ad hoc Compliance projects as necessary
  • Investigate issues arising from compliance surveillance and testing efforts.

Your skills and experience:

  • Excellent knowledge of compliance in alternative/illiquid funds, broader compliance, and regulatory matters, with experience/ knowledge of the financial markets and the current regulatory structure in asset management in HK and Mainland China.
  • Prior experience in Illiquid Investment Fund, the distribution of such funds and knowledge of administering compliance controls for illiquid asset classes (e.g. Sustainable Investment, Private equity, real estate)
  • Experience of building and managing relationships with senior stakeholders and an ability to influence senior stakeholders and colleagues
  • Ability to generate innovative ideas and challenge the status quo
  • Self-disciplined and have the ability to work under pressure and within tight deadlines with no loss of accuracy.
  • Proven analytical skills
  • Strong organizational skills, including an ability to prioritize effectively
  • A minimum of 8 - 10 years asset management Compliance with illiquid asset classes (e.g. SI, Private equity, real estate) experience within a financial institution, regulator and/or similar environment.
  • Preference will be given to candidates with passed AMAC exams.
  • Fluency in English and Mandarin is essential.
