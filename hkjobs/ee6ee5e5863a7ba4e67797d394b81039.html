UBS - Global Markets Compliance Officer


2023-01-28 - Compliance - Hong Kong

Your role

We are looking for a Compliance Ofﬁcer in Hong Kong, who will join the Global Markets Compliance team to:
• Interact regularly with the Global Markets business covering all asset classes, including Fixed Income, OTC and Listed Equity Derivatives, Trading, Sales and Structured Products.
• Provide direction to the Global Markets Business on regulatory requirements related to China Global Markets activities (e.g., Stock Connect, QFII, CIBM, Bond Connect, laws issued by Chinese Regulators) and ensure compliance risks are identiﬁed, reported and tracked to their resolution.
• Provide support and independent and timely guidance and challenge to the business and other functions on all issues relating to the day-to -day business, new business initiatives, regulatory developments, transactional queries, and proposed material amendments to controls and process flow.
• Arrange for the drafting and provision of suitable training to respective business areas within Global Markets, either face to face or via other mediums such as online training.
• Review manuals and compliance notices to ensure they are properly revised and/or updated where necessary and provide the respective desks within Global Markets with the relevant updates and guidance.
• Draft new manuals, Compliance Notices, policies, and procedures in light of regulatory amendments and/or business changes
• Analyze processes and systems and provide guidance to relevant stakeholders on the implementation of improved solutions that support business and mitigate compliance risk.
• Liaise with internal auditors and other control functions on various request in relation to compliance matters.
• Be involved in investigating conduct risk issues, whether instigated internally or externally.

Your team

Your team
You’ll be working in the Global Markets Compliance &
Operational Risk Control (C&ORC) team in Hong Kong based across the Global Markets trading floor, with additional engagement with Global Markets Compliance teams across APAC and Globally.

We support the Global Markets business by partnering with them to effectively manage regulatory risk in an environment of collaboration and constructive challenge. Our role is to provide high quality and professional compliance services in support of the Hong Kong Global Markets business activities. This role will cover all aspects of general compliance duties aligned to Global Markets business.

Your expertise

Your expertise
• Ideally 8-10 years in the financial services industry, internal audit and/or with a securities exchange or regulator
• Sound understanding of applicable rules and regulations relevant to PRC foreign investor access channels, for example, China QFII, China Connect program, Bond Connect and CIBM
• Sound understanding of applicable HKMA and SFC rules and regulations
• Sound know-how of the Investment Bank and Global Markets business and products
• Strong relationship building, communication, interpersonal skills, and analytical skills
• Ability to think out of the box and challenge the status quo to ensure that there is always room for improvement
• Able to critically assess and challenge documentation with an independent mindset
• Reliable, resilient and capable of independently dealing with business desk heads and key stakeholders (challenging situations and conversations don’t sway you away)
• Fluent in both English and Mandarin is necessary to effectively interact with stakeholders. Fluency in Cantonese is also advantageous.
