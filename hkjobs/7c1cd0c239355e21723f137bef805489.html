The role is a key part of the delivery of Operations Data Mesh within the Securities Services Operations team providing support and challenge within operations globally.  The Operations Data Mesh Lead is a champion for the Operations Data Mesh transformation program and must ensure that partners, stakeholders and teams within Operations understand the direction of travel in the pivot to a distributed data-driven organisation, participate in the appropriate programs and deliver on the global work-streams.

This role sits in Digital and Platform Services, supporting Securities Services Operations in APAC & globally, as part of APAC Securities Services Operations and AI & Analytics. The role is based in Hong Kong and will report to the Head of APAC Securities Services operations and the Global Head of Securities Services operations AI & Analytics.

 

Operations Data Mesh Lead Description:

  • One of Operations key strategic goals is to pivot to a distributed data driven organization to support the business in their development of data services in particular and to improve quality, automation, AI deployment and client service in general.
  • In pursuit of this pivot to data, Operations have developed a few key programs and capabilities, including the Operations Data Hub, the Operations Data Academy and AI-enabled digital tooling to support the delivery of data and insight at scale and speed.
  • The next step in the Operations data journey is to adopt Data Mesh as a framework, a way of thinking and mechanism to deliver the distributed data driven organization.  This will be a multi-year journey and will require very close partnership with the business and technology.
  • The Operations Data Mesh Lead is an APAC based role with joint global ownership to design, devise and develop an Operations Data Mesh strategy, roadmap, proof of concepts and a comprehensive adoption and delivery program.

 

  • Focus includes:
    • Devise and develop the Operations Data Mesh strategy in partnership with the business and technology and other key leads in the overall Operations data program â€“ ODH, Data Academy & Data Strategy.
    • Define a framework & roadmap to deliver on the strategy, including definitions, standards, training content, forums and mechanisms to serve a program of work.
    • Develop an Operations Data Mesh toolkit to set out a pathway for adoption of Data Mesh concepts, domains, products and the framework.
    • Socialize and train Operations leads, domain and product owners on the toolkit and initiate a broad, concurrent, distributed program of work to develop domain and data product owner capability.
    • Initiate and lead a series of proof of concept projects to be run concurrent & distributed with data product owners, ODH, technology and data consumers.
    • Driving risk identification and risk mitigation across the programs & across the business to ensure successful implementation of Operations Data Mesh.
    • Set up and operate a set of qualitative and quantitative measure to monitor execution and success of Operations Data Mesh, in particular the success of discovery, addressability and consumption of Operations Data Products.
