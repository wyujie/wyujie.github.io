IT Operations manages the IT infrastructure and supporting services that are critical for HSBC employees and customers, such as cost-efficient hardware, system software, middleware and databases, data centres, voice and data networks, ATMs, desktops and other devices. 

The area comprises five functions: Client Services, Enterprise Services and Architecture, Data Centre and Service Management, Relationship Management, Project Management and Risk and Administration. Collectively, these areas define and deliver HSBC’s technology strategy to support the Group’s goals.

We are currently seeking a high caliber professional to join our team as  Chief Technology Officer, Head of Engineering, Global Operations and Automation Technology .

Overview

Global Operations and Automation exist ‘To enable HSBC with Bankwide process transformation capabilities through resilient and scalable intelligent automation technology and services’.  Our vision is to ‘To be HSBC's preferred intelligent automation single marketplace delivering secure, innovative and scalable technology that enables world-class customer journeys’ The team are truly global: developing and delivering change across all key markets supported by HSBC with a host of leading technologies.  They provide technology ownership of several hundred applications that are integral to the successful support to HSBC Global Operations.  Transformational change remains primary across the current book of work, delivering products and capabilities to enable faster and more streamlined services to customers of the global businesses and functions. 

Role purpose

The role holder will drive innovation and technical excellence across the GO&A IT function not only in the application development space but also in the infrastructure delivery, including defining the strategy, designing, developing, implementing and managing Global Operations and Automation IT infrastructure services, service management and world class engineering practices. This role is responsible for defining and operating the functions’ critical IT Service Management processes such as Change Management, Problem Management, Service Resilience, Capacity Management and Infrastructure Security Management, which are all critical to ensure the ongoing stability of the Global Operations and Automation critical business services.  The role will define and roll-out best-in-class engineering standards including DevSecOps practices in an agile environment and provide practical leadership guidance on cloud adoption and roadmap for current and future GO&A applications.  The role will utilise best practice engineering methods to provide expert technical guidance for engineering initiatives.

The role holder will drive the development of the function’s IT Infrastructure strategy including sourcing and vendor strategies, standards, policies, guidelines and procedures as well as managing implementation at regional level in line with direction from the global CTO. 

The role holder is responsible for managing operational risk within IT Infrastructure and Cloud.  This function will establish robust and tested processes for business continuity and disaster recovery and will ensure the infrastructure components are ever-greened and within the R2 stack and, where appropriate, look toward innovation within the industry to drive value and service quality across the GO&A estate.

They will have overall accountability for the resources employed across the global function with direct responsibility as a representative into Group CTO function regarding IT Infrastructure, service management, cloud adoption and roadmap, devSecOps implementation and engineering standards. 

The role will accelerate and optimize the functions continuous service quality improvements, innovation and optimisation of application development and it’s internal IT infrastructure and externally hosted IT infrastructure.
