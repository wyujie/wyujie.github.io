Macquarie - Manager/Senior Manager – Trade support


2023-02-14 - - Hong Kong

This is a great opportunity for you to join Macquarie Bank’s Corporate Operation Group as a Middle Office Support, where your will start a journey of empowering people to innovate and invest in better future.

The Market Operations division within Corporate Operations Group business is providing business aligned trade support services, such as trade verification, confirmation, settlement, reconciliation, and reporting for wild range of products and currencies for many of Macquarie's businesses globally. The division provides a key segregated control function for wholesale businesses built on strong operational risk management and a control culture that is consistent and resilient.

We are looking for a person in the trade support team to support Volatility trading desk within CGM EDT. The core function of the role is to provide support service to the trading desks and will closely work with internal and external SMEs.

Responsibilities

  • Timely and accurate completion of all daily tasks to ensure correct economics is record in the risk management system, such as trade booking, life-cycle management, corporate action, rate maintenance
  • Problem solving and dynamic working to any issue raised from internal and external SMEs in various aspects, products and markets, including PnL query, settlement issue, funding
  • Automate, streamline process, and innovate ideas to increase the capacity of the team
  • Participant in projects and UATs

Requirements

  • 8 years+ of strong experience in an investment banking environment, preferably middle office or trade processing role
  • Specific knowledge in warrant issuance and equity Asian markets is desirable
  • Strong operate risk mindset and front-to-back knowledge
  • Excellent interpersonal communication skills, capable of working with management at different levels
  • Strong proficiency in MS Excel, VBA, Alteryx knowledge is an advantage
