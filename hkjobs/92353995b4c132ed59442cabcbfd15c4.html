Overview:

DWS Group (DWS) is one of the world's leading asset managers with EUR 859bn of assets under management (as of 30 June 2021). Building on more than 60 years of experience, it has a reputation for excellence in Europe, Americas and APAC. DWS is recognised by clients globally as a trusted source for integrated investment solutions, stability and innovation across a full spectrum of investment disciplines

We offer individuals and institutions access to our strong investment capabilities across all major asset classes and solutions aligned to growth trends. Our diverse expertise in Active, Passive and Alternatives asset management – as well as our deep environmental, social and governance focus--  complement each other when creating targeted solutions for our clients. Our expertise and on-the-ground-knowledge of our economists, research analysts and investment professionals are brought together in one consistent global CIO View, which guides our strategic investment approach

The Product Division is responsible for the whole lifecycle of products and solutions that DWS offers, from the inception of product strategy through implementation and ongoing management.  Key pillars within the Product Division include Product Strategy, Lifecycle & Analytics, Product Specialists & and Product Management. The Product Division is currently looking for a Product Manager (in Singapore or Hong Kong) who will be responsible for maintaining DWS’ onshore and offshore fund offerings. 

Your Key Responsibilities:

  • Lead funds’ related projects and events with local and global teams. These projects and events may concern locally domiciled and offshore domiciled funds and include among others: fund launches, registrations and deregistrations, license applications, liquidations/mergers, fund documentation updates and changes.
  • Lead Product Division’s strategic initiatives, collaborating with other teams such as Product Specialists team, Legal team, Investment Risk team, Compliance team, Portfolio Management team, etc.
  • Coordinate funds’ related local regulatory reports with local and global teams. The local regulatory reports might concern onshore and offshore domiciled funds and include among others: funds’ portfolio information, flows, distributions, updates to local legal documentation and website, funds’ factsheets, financial reports, ad-hoc requests from the regulator.
  • Support local Sales Team for product management related requests. Such activity might involve among others: answering distributors and other clients’ questions about products’ structures.

Your Skills & Experience:

  • Bachelor’s degree level or equivalent qualification/relevant work experience
  • You have at least 8 years of experience with a fund management company, preferably in product management or business management experience
  • Proven ability to build collaborative working relationship with internal and external stakeholders at the global and local level
  • Strong project management and organizational skills, including attention to detail, ability to manage priorities and deliver projects on time in a demanding, fast-paced environment
  • Results-oriented self-starter with the ability to work both independently and as a team member, coupled with the enthusiasm to deliver world-leading solutions for our clients
  • Ability to build strong relationships with a range of teams and individuals and influence outcomes
  • Solution driven and use initiative to propose new, practical strategies/solutions
  • Proficiency in all Microsoft Office applications
  • Knowledge of global fund wrappers (mutual funds and ETFs) preferred
  • Knowledge of MAS or SFC regulations preferred
  • Excellent communication skills in both English & Mandarin written and verbal preferred
