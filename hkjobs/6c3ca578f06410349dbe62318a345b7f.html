Position Overview

Details of the Division and Team:

The trading desk is responsible for pricing and risk managing electronic liquidity on FIC asset classes within GEM. The team builds and manages innovative quantitative pricing and risk management algorithms in order to meet client’s liquidity requirements while generating sustainable commercial results. As part of the role you will partner with Technology to enhance the system and reduce operational risk and you will work closely with various partners in the bank to meet clients’ needs. 

What we will offer you:

A healthy, engaged and well-supported workforce is better equipped to do their best work and, more importantly, enjoy their lives inside and outside the workplace. That’s why we are committed to providing an environment with your development and wellbeing at its center.

You can expect:

  • Competitive Salary and non-contributory pension

  • 20 days annual leave plus generous number of public holidays

  • Life Assurance and Private Healthcare for you and your family

  • A range of flexible benefits including retail discounts, gym benefits, wellbeing incentives

  • The opportunity to support a wide ranging CSR program plus take volunteering leave days 


Your key responsibilities:

  • Building a sustainable e business and market share.

  • Developing and building pricing and hedging algos.

  • Enhancing the existing electronic trading platform with our key partners globally.

  • Implementing analytics to analyse the existing portfolio for better understanding of risk dynamics and identify trading opportunities.

  • Interacting with clients, sales and internal counterparties in other trading, coverage and structuring functions to increase transparency and to generate new businesses.

Your skills and experience:

  • Minimum 6+ years of experience working on an algorithmic/electronic trading desk

  • Solid track record of running an e trading book and developing market making algo strategies

  • Strong knowledge in Fixed Income and Currencies ( FIC ) products is a big plus although we are also open to strong candidates with background from other asset classes

  • Good understanding of market structure and regulatory landscape across Asian / other EM markets

  • Fluency in programming languages such as python, java/C++, kdb+&q is a must

  • Advanced degree in Statistic / Mathematics / Engineering is preferable

Role is required to be performed primarily on-site at the International Commerce Centre.  Relevant health and safety requirements apply.
