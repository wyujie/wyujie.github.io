Barclays - Front Office C#/Python Developer


2023-02-12 - Technology - Hong Kong, Hong Kong China

Front Office C#/Python Developer
Hong Kong

As Barclays C#/Python Developer, this is an exciting opportunity where you will be involved in the design, maintenance and enhancement of the strategic pricing application –SPARX. This is a spreadsheet pricing application, written in FMD (in house functional programming language) and C#. The team overall employs agile techniques and develops software in a variety of languages. Equity derivative business knowledge would be extremely beneficial as you need to work closely with traders to understand the requirements.

Barclays is one of the world's largest and most respected financial institutions, with 329 years of success, quality and innovation behind us. We offer careers that provide endless opportunity – helping millions of individuals and businesses thrive, and creating financial and digital solutions that the world now takes for granted.

What will you be doing?

  • You will gather user requirements and implement different pricers for products ranging from Vanilla products, like equity option/equity swap to light exotic products etc
  • Being innovative and drive key architectural decisions to come up something new and deliver the FOIT Strategy
  • Interacting with different people, Trading/Operations and Finance clients as well as work closely with different region team members
  • You will be involved in the integration of FO IT solutions into other framework where required

What we’re looking for:

  • Have Bachelor’s degree or foreign equivalent in Mathematics, Financial Engineering, Computer Science, or related field
  • Three plus years of technical proficiency with Have 3+ years of hands on development experience with one or more of C#, Python, VBA.
  • Have hands on development experience with databases design & SQL languages
  • Be proficient in software development process, knowledge of data structures, algorithms and general problem solving skills and best practices (TDD, code reviews etc.)

Skills that will help you in the role:

  • Knowledge of Exotic Equities Derivatives financial products and related risk measures and pricing
  • Experience in automation using scripting languages including JavaScript, VB and/or Batch File Scripting
  • Proven ability to pick up and learn new languages on the job.

Where will you be working?

Our offices are situated in the center of Hong Kong’s financial district, at 2 Queen’s Road.
