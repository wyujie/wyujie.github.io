About The Role:
Morgan Stanley’s Securitized Products Group Lending Asia (SPGLA) focuses on traditional commercial real estate lending as well as warehouse lending for pools of loan assets including ABS (residential, consumer, auto/equipment), middle market and corporate loans as well as commercial real estate loans. Morgan Stanley’s Fixed Income Division is recognized for providing traditional and innovative financing techniques, helping a wide range of clients in Australia and Asia to execute their business strategy. We are looking to recruit an experienced Execution Lawyer to work on all aspects of real estate and securitisation financing. This role will include working with originators and syndication/securitisation teams to help structure and execute transactions with involvement from initial underwriting stage through to exit of the risk position. This is an on-desk role, which will put the individual at the forefront of the structuring and documentation of financing transactions. It is a varied and exciting role with plenty of scope to develop within a busy and growing team.

 

Key Responsibilities:
Main responsibilities centre on working with the core underwriting teams on commercial real estate and securitised lending transactions, including:

  • Preparing and reviewing term sheets
  • Advising on structure of transactions taking into account legal, regulatory, tax and other considerations
  • Review of due diligence
  • Liaising with external counsel to prepare transaction documentation
  • Liaising with internal stakeholders including Legal, Tax, Compliance and Ops teams
  • Acting as a key point of contact for origination/distribution/capital markets teams

Other responsibilities include:

  • Assisting with team to process non-disclosure agreements, engagement letters, reliance letters, KYC and conflicts process
  • Preparing and updating precedents and templates
  • Helping to co-ordinate know-how/training for commercial real estate and securitisation financing related matters and across the team generally

 

Qualifications

  • At least 5+ PQE City or equivalent banking law experience with a major law firm or investment bank
  • Familiarity with LMA documentation in respect of real estate financing transactions in Australia and Asia (China & India)
  • Commercially driven with strong structuring experience; ability to adapt to varied and challenging workload
