Linux Low Latency Systems Engineer

Latency Critical Trading (LCT) seeks a Linux Systems Engineer to join our team.

The LCT Team is responsible for delivering high-quality low latency trading capabilities for Millennium’s businesses via core infrastructure, software development, automation, security, and cloud-based services. An ideal candidate should have hands-on experience in the Linux environment with knowledge spanning across Virtualization technologies, Open Source software running on Linux, third-party software and Cloud technologies.

Team Composition

Our team consists of highly skilled C++, Linux, Network, SRE, and other highly skilled Linux Engineers equipped with the tools and untethered access required to make meaningful changes to both general purpose and niche server environments. We pride ourselves in providing a stable and high performing platform to our users which include portfolio managers, researchers, and other technologists. A great deal of focus is spent on continuously learning, consistently making improvements, and teamwork.

Job Function Summary

As a senior member of the team, the candidate will work closely with the heads of multiple infrastructure and application support teams to build and operate an ultra-low latency trading platform from the ground up. The engineer will be responsible for the training and guidance of other team members on low latency/high performance computing concepts and serve as a leader within the team. While the role focuses primarily on Linux servers, the candidate will have access to the full technology stack to troubleshoot and preemptively identify potential issues.

Principal Responsibilities

  • Part of the globally deployed engineering team that supports and maintains Virtualization/Linux and related infrastructure stack
  • Identifying, prioritizing, and resolving high level incidents and requests
  • Proactively working on issues, identifying problem areas, and providing solutions to enhance quality of service and to prevent future problems
  • Requirement gathering, analyzing, and Implementing the solutions as per the business requirements
  • Identifying risks, forming contingency plans, and implementing them
  • Collaborating with engineers, developers, and other technologists to find solutions to complex problems while working closely and supporting other technical and business teams for their day to day requirements
  • Delivering stable, repeatable, and high performing Linux systems through code
  • Monitoring the performance and health of the latency critical computing environment
  • Investigating performance related issues using various tools; eg: systemtap
  • Performing production changes and deployments
  • Leading discussions and ensure consistent communication with involved parties and stakeholders
  • Producing quality documentation of the low latency environment
  • Participating in off-hours, weekends and holidays on-call support as and when required

  • 4+ years of experience in low latency trading systems

  • Ability to communicate at detailed levels to teams and users
  • Ability to thrive in a dynamic, fast-paced environment with an independent/self-starter work ethic
  • Quick learner, Detail oriented; Demonstrable thoroughness and strong ownership of work
