Point 72 - Market Risk Analyst


2023-02-11 - Risk - New York   |   Stamford   |   Hong Kong   |   Singapore   |   Paris

Role

Point72 Asset Management is seeking a Market Risk Analyst to join its Risk & Quantitative Research team.

The Risk team plays a vital role in the Firm’s investment process, building a deeply rooted culture of efficient risk management and factful performance attribution. The Market Risk Analyst will review portfolio risks and performance, support the development of comprehensive risk reports for Management and Portfolio Managers, and be an important contributor to driving improvements to the existing risk management system, with the goal of helping the firm deliver superior risk-adjusted performance. The paramount mission of the team is to protect the Firm from improper levels of exposure and ensure that risk-taking is always efficient and deliberate.

The ideal candidate is hardworking, self-motivated, and detail oriented who can articulate both market risk management and systems concepts effectively to a diverse audience in a fast-paced environment.  The role will cover a wide range of assets, but the candidate must have experience covering global macro products (fixed income, credit, FX).   


The Market Risk Analyst will:

  • Analyze firm portfolios to identify market risks and performance drivers, expand the current risk infrastructure to facilitate efficient risk management, and ensure comprehensive coverage of firm risk exposures.
  • Support senior risk managers and colleagues on topics such as risk limit usage, firm and portfolio risk exposures, tail risks, and forward-looking risk events.
  • Demonstrate a strong understanding of foundational market risk management concepts, such as model Greeks, historical stress testing, value-at-risk, and liquidity measurement.  
  • Help design and prototype risk analytics and reports for portfolios of diverse products and strategies. 
  • Collaborate closely with the technology team to deploy prototypes to production; continuously enhance reports and systems areas when necessary.
  • Work with other groups (Operations, Finance, Treasury, Compliance) to strengthen data integration, develop firm reports, and to produce collaborative analysis for management.
  • Evaluate reference data and vendor analytics packages and work to integrate them into the risk system. Remediate data issues, QA new features and isolate issues in existing risk system in conjunction with risk technology.

We seek candidates with:

  • An undergraduate degree with a strong academic record. An advanced degree in finance, statistics, or engineering is desirable.
  • 3+ years of work experience in market risk or trading capacity related to global macro and fixed income, solid product knowledge and analytical rigor in terms of pricing models, risk sensitivities and best practices for risk aggregation in a portfolio context
  • High level of proficiency working with large data sets and databases is required (SQL proficiency is highly desirable).  Experience with data science programming languages (Python, MATLAB, R) is a plus. 
  • Strong communication skills with prior experience interacting with risk managers, senior management, and back-office personnel.
  • The ability to independently manage multiple tasks and deadlines in a fast-paced environment.
  • The ability to work cooperatively with all levels of staff as part of a team.
  • Experience with MSCI/RiskMetrics or other market risk vendor platforms in a production environment.
  • Experience with pricing function packages (i.e., FinCad, QuantLib).
  • A commitment to the highest ethical standards and to act with professionalism and integrity
