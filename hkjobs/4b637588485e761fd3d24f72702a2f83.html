Job Description


The Role:

Citadel is looking for an exceptional Operations Associate to liaise with trading desks and counterparties to assist in the lifecycle of a trade.  This includes settlements, confirmations, and reconciliations.  The Operations Associates add value by developing new tools, applications and processes to support a wide-ranging array of current and new businesses, while demonstrating an intellectual curiosity and passion for innovation. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in OTC and Physical commodities operations. Experience with Physical Natural Gas settlements is an advantage.  The role will be to support physical natural gas, power, metals, environmental products, and weather trading.  The successful candidate will be a team player with the ability to manage many relationships, both within the firm and with outside counterparties.


Key Responsibilities:


  • Manage monthly settlement cycle with trading counterparties, pipelines, and storage facilities.

  • Manage physical commodities trade confirmation process.

  • Work closely with the trading desk, scheduler, and counterparties to ensure all details are accurately reported in our systems.

  • Perform maintenance on trades and positions, namely amendments and deletions.

  • Monitor and improve reconciliations between trading systems and back office books and records.

  • Monitor and improve reconciliations between the back office books and records and external counterparties.

  • Consistently strive to improve all processes.

  • Ask thoughtful questions, constantly strive for improvement, and challenge the status quo.



Skills & Requirements:


  • Bachelor’s degree with a cumulative GPA of 3.5+ or equivalent grade.

  • 3+ years of professional experience in financial services, or other operational or quantitative fields (i.e.: engineering, mathematics, manufacturing, etc.) working with commodities products.

  • Experience in structured commodities products, a strong plus.

  • Motivated, self-starter, with ability to multi-task and work independently.

  • Capable of project managing, innovating, and driving projects to completion.

  • Proficient in Microsoft Excel (i.e.: formulas, v-lookups, pivot tables) and data analysis.



Location: Greenwich, CT
