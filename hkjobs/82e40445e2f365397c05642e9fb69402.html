Barclays - QPS Embedded C++ Developer


2023-02-12 - Technology - Hong Kong, Hong Kong China

QPS Embedded FPGA/ C++ Developer
Hong Kong

As a Barclays QPS Embedded FPGA/ C++ Developer, you will be joining a team focused on building out the functionality and technology of the SubM Native systems for our flagship product offering for giving ultra-low latency to a very niche domain of very high volume and low latency sophisticated Equites trading clients, mostly Hedge funds. This is an exciting opportunity to interact with both senior Front Office Trading and Sales stakeholders at the Director and Managing Director level across multiple regions.

Barclays is one of the world's largest and most respected financial institutions, with 329 years of success, quality and innovation behind us. We offer careers that provide endless opportunity – helping millions of individuals and businesses thrive, and creating financial and digital solutions that the world now takes for granted.

Hybrid Working
We are currently operating in a hybrid working environment, meaning that many colleagues spend part of their working hours at home and part in the office, depending on the nature of the role they are in.  Please discuss the detail of the working pattern options for the role with the hiring manager.

What will you be doing?

  • Design and development of industry-leading, ultra low-latency Equities trading systems and taking up the full responsibility of the multiple deliveries at a time.
  • Testing and holistic simulation of the trading system for high reliability
  • Providing estimates for tasks including design, development, and testing
  • Supporting analysis, testing, and operations teams
  • Participating in code reviews and architectural decisions
  • Managing stakeholders and displaying leadership qualities
  • Solving problems and making appropriate judgement calls
  • Mentoring junior developers

What we’re looking for:

  • Industrial experience with Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs) design and verification in SystemVerilog, Verilog, or VHDL
  • Excellent Embedded C++ and above, programming skills with strong knowledge of STL, Boosts and GTest
  • Good scripting skills using Python, Perl or similar
  • Understanding of higher-level Computer Science concepts such as data structures and algorithms

Skills that will help you in the role:

  • Proven experience and understanding in Investment banking, working with directly with Traders and Desk Heads
  • Advanced expertise in computer networks, computer architecture, operating systems specially knowledge of Linux and Agile software development methodology
  • Industry experience on Java is a plus
  • Degree in Computer Engineering, or Computer Science

Where will you be working?
Our offices are situated in the center of Hong Kong’s financial district, at 2 Queen’s Road.
Cheung Kong Centre is an investment banking hub that offers panoramic views of Victoria Harbour and Kowloon Peninsula. There is a variety of local amenities and you will be just a short walk from shopping malls, health clubs and multiple transport links.
