J.P. Morgan - UHNW Single Coverage Banker, China- Vice President, Wealth Management


2023-02-01 - Client Advisory - Hong Kong

Responsibilities

  • Support and manage all aspects of client relationships across the team; develop and maintain long-term relationships through professional/personal networks and contacts
  • Operate in both banker and investor capacity, and handle/coordinate with various teams on both overall account inquiries/maintenance as well as on investment-related aspects
  • Articulate and deliver strategic messages to their portfolio, prepare presentations and proposals, and take both solicited and unsolicited orders in all asset classes
  • Update clients regularly on markets, portfolio performance, investments opportunities, risks
  • Partner with the client including (but not limited to) updating KYC information, client suitability profile, hosting client meetings, monitoring funds/asset transfer and handling client ad-hoc queries
  • Collaborate with the other integrated team members to provide a high quality of client service
  • Keeping track of Clients, AUMs and Revenues using our internal systems; periodic calculation of account performance
  • Work together with other teams in meeting all compliance, regulatory, risk management, operational, and due diligence requirements and compliance with internal policies

 Qualifications

  • 5+ years of experience in the Financial Services and/or Banking industry. Prior experience as a client advisor is a plus
  • Ideally understand and have a working knowledge of the private banking key regulatory and control environments that are relevant to the team and be able to manage the accounts accordingly
  • Proven client management skills and effective communication skills (oral and written) who can positively engage and influence clients as well as internal stakeholders
  • Strong organizational and prioritization skills
  • Operate with a high level of integrity and risk awareness
  • Must be a team player and able to work with different business functions across the bank
  • As the role is an important contributor to the strategy of JPM, comfort and experience working with and reporting to senior management is required
  • Appropriate local regulatory qualification and licenses
  • Fluency in English mandatory, Cantonese and/or Mandarin strongly preferred
