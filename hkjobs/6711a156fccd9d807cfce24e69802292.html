Macquarie - Diversity, Equity & Inclusion Lead, Asia


2023-02-14 - - Multiple locations

As a passionate DE&I professional, you will lead the execution and design of our DE&I strategy across Asia. Working closely with regional management, Country Heads, DE&I peers, HR Business Partners and Country HR teams and our incredible Employee Network Groups, you will be responsible for driving one of the most important aspects of workplace culture for our business.

This role is perfect for an experienced DE&I professional and change ambassador who can lead the delivery of meaningful strategies and innovative programs that deliver our Diversity, Equity & Inclusion objectives. You will partner with key stakeholders and provide advice and support to our Asia CEO and Regional Diversity, Equity & Inclusion Committee, leveraging insights from market data, emerging trends, and best practice globally. You are a natural consultant who can align equitable processes, inclusive practices, goals, and behaviours with business outcomes.

You will be taking ownership for the delivery of a range of regional DE&I initiatives, high-profile events and supporting our Employee Network Groups to connect across the Region and the Group to facilitate the sharing of ideas.

At Macquarie we are extremely proud of our culture and diversity – you will be partnering with our communications and recruitment teams to help promote Macquarie’s Diversity, Equity & Inclusion commitments externally as well as working with and managing relationships with external DE&I partners.

This is a fantastic opportunity for someone who loves strategic thinking, building and executing programs, with proven project management experience and the skills to translate data into action. Strong communication, influencing and negotiating skills with an ability to communicate across a range of mediums and to a variety of audiences will also be important to your success. 

Experience in the area of Diversity, Equity and Inclusion is preferred, along with experience driving organisational or cultural change in Asia.

Our commitment to Diversity, Equity and Inclusion
