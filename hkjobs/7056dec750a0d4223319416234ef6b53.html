  • Solve problems by identifying and guiding technology solution

  • Experience working for or with large technology organizations

  • Outstanding communication skills – experience working with both development teams and stakeholders/clients

  • Effective with prioritization/time management, especially within a fast-paced environment

  • Hands on coding experience with Python / R, SQL

  • Bachelor’s degree required, preferably in Mathematics, Information Systems, Computer Science, or related field

  • Management consulting and/or product management experience at a top firm strongly preferred

  • Experience with project management tools (Jira/Confluence) preferred
