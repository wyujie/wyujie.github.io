UBS - Group Shareholding Reporting Specialist


2023-01-28 - Compliance, Legal, Risk - Hong Kong

Your role

Are you familiar with regulatory reporting? Do you have the know-how to analyze global holdings? The Group Shareholding Reporting (GSR) team is looking for someone like that to help us:

• discharge UBS’s global obligations for reporting of aggregated shareholdings in accordance with regulatory and legal requirements in each of the relevant jurisdictions it operated
• understand the different Business Divisions (Wealth Management, Investment Bank and Asset Management) activities to be able to assess their relevance for reporting
• place and monitor trading restrictions and provide trade approvals to business when questions of regulatory ownership limits arise
• provide regulatory guidance to business functions and liaise with the Legal Department if necessary to interpret unclear regulation
• promote and raise awareness of GSR related topics and the department in general through interactions across the Group
• establish and maintain good relationships with Regulators, Exchanges, other areas within the bank and colleagues based in other jurisdictions
• guide less experienced members of the wider GSR APAC team in execution of the GSR mandate

Your team

• You’ll be a member of the APAC HK hub of Group Shareholding Reporting Team in Group Compliance, Regulatory & Governance function located in Hong Kong. The APAC unit is responsible for analyzing global equity holdings in Issuers listed in APAC jurisdictions, monitoring holdings in sensitive industries and where appropriate, reporting holdings to the relevant regulators. This role includes providing compliance advisory support to trading desks, interpreting new regulations, implementing monitoring processes to ensure compliance, improving internal controls, assessing strengths and weaknesses in the GSR programs and actively seeking and promoting opportunities to develop and update processes and / or efficiencies.

Your expertise

• Bachelor’s degree (university of applied sciences), ideally in Law, Economics or Business Administration
• good knowledge of Financial Markets, Banking Industry and products (prior Banking, Asset or Fund Management experience is an advantage)
• intensive attention to detail and the ability to analyze and process large amounts of data
• experience of process management and a good understanding of the need to mitigate process risks
• extensive MS Excel skills
• fluency in English
You are:
• willing to perform work to a constantly high level of accuracy
• a great communicator that can handle challenging situations
• enquiring in nature with a propensity to question facts and data, and a tenacious attitude towards problem solving
• self-motivated and willing to always go that step further
• an excellent team player, ambitious and structured
