UBS - Client Advisor, SCSD HK


2023-01-28 - Client advisory / Relationship management - Hong Kong

Your role

Are you risk alert? Are you a strategic, solution-oriented thinker and an excellent team player? Do you have a proven track record in dealing with challenging conversations?

We’re looking for a Client Advisor to join our Special Client Situations Desk HK which involves compliance/strategic, issues as well as clients whom the Bank has deemed to be outside our target market.

We are looking for someone who:
• Likes challenges, is self-motivated, independent, innovative, and able to think strategically with a clear mind under pressure
• Is organized, meticulous, and able to handle large volumes of client servicing and meet deadlines
• Has an acute sense of risk management and displays a high-standard, risk-based approach when dealing with complex issues
• Ensure plausibility of the instructions received from clients; review, identify and assess risks associated to transactions and/or status changes that are emerged in the existing relationship
• Is empathetic, collaborative, eager to build knowledge in dealing with all clients’ special situations across all markets

Your team

You’ll be working in the Special Client Situation Desk within Wealth Management Team in Hong Kong to support UBS in achieving its strategic, operational and compliance objectives. Our mission is to be an effective front-line desk which protects the Bank’s business by delivering an excellent client experience as well as executing a high-quality risk management for all banking relationships assigned to SCSD team.

Your expertise

• At least 5 years of client facing experience including as a private banker or assistant private banker
• A high standard of risk management approach for all complex issues including handling sensitive communications
• Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, excellent in dealing and handling challenge situations and clients
• Has a good grasp of banking products (including legacy ones), general regulatory frameworks and compliance guidelines for all markets in UBS AG - Hong Kong & Singapore
• Independent, diligent and effective in prioritization and exit management of client’s book
• Fluent in English, Cantonese and Mandarin
