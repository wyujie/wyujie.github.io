Principal Responsibilities

Provide Jade Universal Life Insurance clients, their representatives and the relationship managers with high level of active day-to-day customer service in all interactions with HSBC Insurance while ensuring that:

  • All necessary documentation as required is received for each case and is properly completed
  • All  administrative and compliance  processes  are  observed as to timeliness, quality, regulation and procedure
  • Cases are processed and all primary records are correctly captured and maintained
  • Underwriting and other case evidence and documentary requirements are coordinated and handled promptly, including the arranging of such requirements as appropriate
  • That policies, endorsements, correspondence and any other case outputs are issued in accordance with required HSBC Insurance standards and regulation
  • Policy information is kept up-to-date through the policy lifecycle
