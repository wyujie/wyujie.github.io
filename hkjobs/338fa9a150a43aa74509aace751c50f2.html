As an Performance and Ex-ante Risk Reporting Associate, you will be responsible to manage client reporting deliveries, drive client change and strategic initiatives for our APAC Investment Information Services ("IIS") business.

This role will require performance and ex-ante risk technical skills, and demonstrated internal/external stakeholder management skills.

Responsibilities:

  • Manage daily client reporting deliveries in accordance to agreed SLA
  • Manage client queries and prioritisation of change requests by working closely with our business partners on the triage, analysis, build and implementation
  • Coordinate regional initiatives to identify operational and system gaps within current processes, and drive automated solutions with applicable controls and work practices
  • Provide oversight governance on offshored functions and monitor performance of service provider on monthly basis
  • Ability to work with multiple vendors in building servicing capabilities and provide operational view on required implementation
  • Partner with APAC Product and Business Leads to create an integrated service platform covering portfolio ex-ante risk, mandate compliance, ESG and performance reporting.
  • Support client RFP by working with internal stakeholders and required SME representation
  • Management of regional and global projects by leading APAC implementation from initial design to execution/implementation of the project life-cycle (including system/vendor migration, testing and implementation)
  • Act as team escalation and ensure timely notification to APAC IIS senior management 
  • Mentor and train staff to strengthen our operational skills and client experience

Requirements:

  • 5-10 years fund performance and ex-ante risk experience (additional exposure to mandate compliance monitoring will be an advantage)
  • Minimum Bachelor's degree in related discipline
  • Strong experience in performance and ex-ante risk (knowledge of mandate compliance and ESG reporting an advantage)
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills in managing senior regional and global stakeholders
  • Experience in similar functions in managing complex business processes, system change and client build requirements
  • Worked with control and risk stakeholders with strong understanding of operational controls, processes and requirements
  • Ability to work in high-paced environment, flexible and be able to organize work priorities for APAC region
  • Fluent in business English (both written and oral) and Mandarin language requirement
