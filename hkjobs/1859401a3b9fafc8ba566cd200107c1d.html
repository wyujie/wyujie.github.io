Jane Street - Accountant, Accounting


2023-01-16 - Finance - Hong Kong

About the Position

We are looking for an Accountant to join our Hong Kong team, supporting both our local and global accounting operations at Jane Street.

Your primary responsibility will be to contribute to the maintenance of a robust financial control for the firm. You’ll work alongside team members to maintain the company’s books and records and help with our month-end close process. This will include the preparation of various reconciliations and financial schedules as we prepare financial statements.

This role will provide a great introduction and exposure to the larger operations of the firm. The right person must be discreet, trustworthy, and comfortable working in a deadline-driven environment.

Getting the details right in accounting is an extremely important part of our business, and we take pride in our ability to stay on schedule without sacrificing accuracy. Each member of our team is highly organised, proactive, and able to prioritise tasks effectively.

The legal and regulatory environment in which we operate is constantly shifting. Your previous industry expertise will be valuable to the team as we collaborate with internal and external stakeholders on accounting policy decisions and applications of IFRS and other relevant GAAPs. A good understanding of the financial markets around the region will be critical to excel in this role.

About You

  • Qualified accountant with 3+ years of relevant work experience
  • Background in financial services is desired but not essential
  • Strong foundation of financial statements production and legal entity reporting
  • In-depth knowledge of IFRS
  • Highly organised with exceptional attention to detail
  • Ability to handle competing priorities and to work effectively in a challenging, fast-paced environment
  • Supportive and reliable team player with a collaborative mindset
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
  • Motivated to improve processes where possible
  • Proficient knowledge of Microsoft Excel
  • Experience with Workday and VBA skills would be a plus
  • Fluency in English required
