Equities Junior Analyst

We are looking for highly skilled people with original ideas to join our Millennium Junior Equity Analyst Training Program in summer of 2023. Analysts will start their first year at UBS being trained by UBS Senior Research Analysts while working as Junior Associates on their teams. The training program would entail:

Comprehensive training in valuation and accounting from UBS' Team

Rotations through multiple coverage verticals (e.g. Consumer, Tech, Industrials, Energy, Financials) to understand different business models, industry drivers and approaches to valuation

Exposure to the UBS trading desk in addition the research/specialist sales teams

 During the second year of training , analyst will be placed on a Millennium Equities Portfolio Management Team and will:

  • Conduct fundamental financial analysis to assess investment opportunities
  • Build and maintain sophisticated financial models and valuation analyses
  • Explore research avenues such as earnings calls/ transcripts, public filings and relevant data
  • Analyze and maintain market data, industry data, and alternative data 


Collaborate closely with sell-side and other buy-side analysts and help manage information flow

  • Communicate due diligence findings with investment team and provide recommendations
  • Strengthen coverage and industry knowledge by attending industry events/conferences and speaking with third party professionals and consultants

Qualifications/Skills Required

  • Bachelor’s degree candidate in their senior year with a strong academic record
  • Candidates must be able to commence full-time employment in summer of 2023 and graduate between January 2023 and May 2023 with a strong academic record
  • Self-directed, detail-oriented problem solvers with a desire to contribute to Millennium’s success
  • Strong passion for financial services industry and investing
  • Ability to prioritize in a fast paced, high pressure, constantly evolving environment
  • Must be thorough, tenacious and resourceful
  • Outstanding verbal and written communication skills
  • Proven ability to contribute and manage time efficiently while understanding urgency 
  • Strong Excel skills
  • Financial modeling experience
  • Demonstration of strong ownership of work
