The Asia Private Banking Client Service Team is currently seeking a dynamic and dedicated professional to join their team. A Client Service Associate (“CSA”) is the primary contact for all service related needs of a Private Bank Client. A CSA works in a team and is responsible to provide support to Bankers and Investors by working with various product partners and operations to deliver a seamless and integrated experience to clients across all Private Bank products.

Job Responsibilities

  • Acts as primary contact with external clients and Integrated team for day to day account administration and servicing
  • Provides proactive personalized service on transaction processing such as deposits, loans, asset transfers, execution of cash products, cash flow management, corporate action, servicing inquiries and investigation handling etc
  • Assists Clients in all account maintenance related requests such as change of address, updated KYC changes and ensures all necessary documentation is processed in accordance with Bank’s standards
  • Proactively outreach to client for digital adoption
  • Assists Bankers in execution of credit facility, facilitates ad-hoc client requests, trouble shooting and help drives any remediation or campaign that is required from Bank

Personal Attributes & Requirements 

Essential Experiences

  • At least 5 years of solid Client Service / Relationship Manager support experience from Private Bank
  • Good working knowledge of all Private Banking products including relevant operational processes on cash management, KYC document review, corporate action and banking products etc
  • High sensitivity to Private Banking Control standards particularly those related to money transfers, securities transfer, credit and any other transactional services
  • Good interpersonal skills on servicing and ability to maintain and deepen relationships
  • Good oral and written communication skills (in English, Cantonese and Mandarin), given the role is required to support Chinese speaking clients
  • Capable of interacting with clients directly and able to resolve client inquiries and trouble shoot problems independently
  • Knowledge of all relevant systems and software packages including Outlook, Excel, Adobe Acrobat and other information sources
  • Fast paced, good teamwork, adaptable, multi-tasking and able to work in a demanding and unpredictable environment
  • Detail-oriented, think out of box and result-oriented

Desirable Experiences

  • At least 5 years of solid Client Service / Relationship Manager support experience from Private Bank

Technical Skills

  • Knowledge of all relevant systems and software packages including e-mail, Excel, Adobe Acrobat and other information sources
