Macquarie - Recruitment Consultant Asia, Hong Kong/Singapore


2023-02-14 - - Multiple locations

Are you an experienced recruiter looking to step up in your career and join a high performing recruitment team (Hudson RPO integrated on-site at Macquarie)? Join our Team, based in our Hong Kong or Singapore office and take responsibility for recruitment across Asia.

You’ll join an expert team of knowledgeable recruiters who partner with the business and work collaboratively in a team environment to deliver world class recruitment services to our stakeholders.

In this role you will source, attract, and assess the very best talent in the market, building credibility and driving trust as you deliver in this role. You will have prior in-house recruitment experience within Financial Services (Risk, Compliance, Finance, Operations, Audit and Tech). Relevant sector experience with an agency or executive search firm environment will also be considered.

We need you to be an experienced and established Recruitment Consultant or Talent Acquisition Specialist who thrives when given accountability. You are adaptable, as this is a fast-paced and complex environment that is constantly changing. You will have a client service ethic that is second to none, a proven capability in delivering innovative attraction and sourcing strategies and a strong background in financial services recruitment.

This is an exceptional opportunity for you to work with a dynamic, growing business. 

To be successful in this role you will be familiar with a variety of sourcing channels such as direct advertising, LinkedIn Recruiter, internal mobility, social media, referrals, and CRM databases to source for active and passive candidates. You’ll also need to demonstrate the ability to work well in a team and be organized, as well as the ability to prioritize your workload efficiently.   Lastly, you will be driven, self-motivated and passionate about delivering best-in-class service.   

You will be able to grow and advance your career, working closely with senior stakeholders to become their trusted recruitment partner.
