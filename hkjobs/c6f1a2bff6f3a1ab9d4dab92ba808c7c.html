J.P. Morgan - Corporate and Investment Banking - Securities Services Operations - Hong Kong - 2023 ReEntry Program


2023-01-24 - Seasonal Employee - Kwun Tong, Kowloon, Hong Kong

The ReEntry Program is a 15-week fellowship program, beginning March 20, 2023 and ending June 30, 2023, with the prospect of an offer for permanent employment with JP Morgan Chase at the end of the program. The program offers a reintroduction to corporate life for those returning to the workplace after an extended career break for two or more years. The fellowship placements will be based on both business needs and candidate skill set and interest areas within our Corporate Investment Banking, Asset Wealth Management & Commercial Banking Operations business. 

Please refer to our ReEntry Overview page for further information regarding the Program

Responsibilities:

As part of the ReEntry Program, you will be assigned to one of the following Operations teams within our Securities Services business.

  • Fund Services Operations
    • The Fund Services Operation Services Delivery (OSD)/ Client Reporting Team deliver a range of services to institutional investors based around the Asian region.  The team is responsible for the production and delivery of client reporting and related accounting/portfolio information. The OSD team is responsible for the day-to-day service management of clients; ensuring queries are answered, problems are resolved and issues are escalated. Act as the point of escalation for the clients, a broad knowledge of fund accounting is required together with sound experience of managing clients
    • The Financial Reporting Team produces and validates a suite of client customized reports timely and accurately and take full ownership for all reporting activities, hub governance and project management of service change request.
    • The Transfer Agency Team provide daily oversight of Transfer Agency Dealing/Pricing/Dividend/Registration/Reconciliation/FIG activities covering Luxembourg, Irish and Cayman domiciled funds distributed by client fund promoters in Asia.  This team is also responsible to process AML/KYC assessments for new account opening and ongoing requirements for account maintenance within applicable deadlines.
    • The Investment Information Services Team manage daily client reporting deliveries in accordance to agreed SLA and client queries and prioritisation of change requests by working closely with our business partners on the triage, analysis, build and implementation.
  • Intelligent Digital and Data Operations
    • Digital and Data Operations team is responsible for driving digital transformation using Automation (RPA, AI, ML) and Data Analytics using tools like Tableau, Alteryx, UiPath etc. to drive operational efficiency and reduce operational risks.  The candidate will be working on various projects to automate manual processes. We are seeking a candidate with experience in change/transformation projects as business/technical/data analysts or project management. The candidate will be working on operating model/process design, solution design, development and delivery of projects using various digital tools.  
  • Custody Client Operations
    • Custody Client Operations team is a client facing operations team that serves as the first point of contact for all post trade activities for the Global Custody business. The candidate will be given training on global custody products (e.g. Trade, FX, Cash, Asset Servicing) and process flows. Main responsibility will include covering client enquiries and report analysis; interacting with various operations teams and stakeholders to gather inputs and feedbacks; analyze and identify opportunities for process improvement; enhance control, and to evaluate the impact to the business. Though this, an extensive network within the firm can be established for ongoing development
  • Investment Middle Office Services
    • Investment Middle Office Services (IMOS) team provide a complete suite of investment operations outsourcing services for institutional asset managers. Our services encompass all asset management activities post-trade execution, allowing asset managers to focus on their core competencies of managing assets and client relationships. The Client Ops Team is the single-point-of-contact for all client interactions including query management, escalations and projects to help clients navigating the firm. The team also partners with internal stakeholders to ensure all client SLA’s are met with high standard of services, monitoring resiliency and focusing on trend analysis and process improvement.

 Requirements:

  • Good analytical and problem solving skills.
  • Maintain high accuracy and thoroughness when executing tasks.
  • Attention to detail
  • Ability to be flexible and work in a dynamic environment  
  • Financial reporting, audit or fund accounting experience will be a plus for Fund Services Operations Team
  • Good understanding of security types and their accounting treatment. Experience of IFRS / IAS is an advantage.
  • Fluency in written & spoken English & Mandarin; Thai/ Korean/ Japanese language skill is an advantage.
  • Willing to work additional hours at peak reporting times if necessary.

Applications close on 29 January 2023 - Individuals must be on a voluntary career break for at least two years in order to be considered for this program.
