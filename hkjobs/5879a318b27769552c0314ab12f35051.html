Global Private Bank is more than just crunching numbers. From the beginning, you will be joining a team committed to building client relationships and making meaningful contributions to our business. With your talent and creativity, you will develop customized wealth management solutions to help some of the world's wealthiest individuals and families grow their wealth.

The J.P. Morgan Private Bank Challenge (JPBC) puts you right in the middle of the action. JPBC offers students a fun, interactive and professional learning experience whilst getting a unique first-hand experience of the firm’s culture. Through a hypothetical case study, you'll find out what it takes to work as an Analyst in Global Private Bank. 

The competition is only open to:

  • Students enrolled in a Bachelor’s Degree Program graduating between December 2024 and July 2025 in Universities located in Singapore, Mainland China, Hong Kong SAR, China or Taiwan, China.
    OR
  • Students enrolled in a Bachelor’s Degree Program graduating between December 2024 and July 2025 with a valid Right-To-Work (Citizen/Permanent Residence only) in Singapore, Mainland China, Hong Kong SAR, China or Taiwan, China.
  • Students should apply to only one location according to their home or study location. 

Team Composition

  • Students must register in teams of strictly 4 members for same location. Once a team has submitted their application, no modification in its composition will be allowed. In the event that the team is reduced to less than 4 Team Members, the team shall be disqualified. 

Application Process

Submit your application through this portal – each Team Member must individually register on the J.P. Morgan website with a CV/Resume uploaded; as such, there should be four individual applications for each team. Please ensure and check that all 4 team member’s graduation date on your application falls between December 2024 and July 2025.

Upon successful registration, your team will be sent a copy of the Terms & Conditions document for the competition, where you will need to review and sign the form. More instructions will be provided at a later stage. Failure to receive the necessary legal documents will result in automatic disqualification of the competition.

Participants who perform well may have the opportunity to have their application prioritized for review in consideration for our upcoming summer internships in our Singapore or Hong Kong offices.

Application Deadline

February 26, 2023 (23:59 Hong Kong/Singapore time)*
*There will be strictly no exceptions for late submissions
