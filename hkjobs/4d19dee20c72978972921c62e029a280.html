Trade Floor Desktop Support

Hong Kong

As a Barclays Trade Floor Desktop Support, you will have impact on Front Office users. It is a highly technical role and requires the right level of skill set. You will join The candidate will join a team dedicated to ensuring the smooth running of desktop systems, applications, provide expert advice and an exceptional level of customer service to business users.

What will you be doing?

  • Providing day-to-day, client facing, technical, infrastructure, application, and systems support to the Trade Floor, monitoring Major Incident reports, keeping aware of outages that affect users
  • Interacting with GTIS/business/RTB/BTB areas to ensure all changes are implemented with minimal impact on the user community
  • Staying prepared to quickly resolve or delegate any possible issue a user can have with technology, while adhering to risk management and best practices
  • Liaising between the Business, and IT teams like Market Data, Messaging, RTB and in-house developer teams to bring about speedy resolution
  • Ensuring 100% start-of-day workstation readiness,  especially after maintenance weekends, ensuring Best Practices are followed, prioritizing based on impact
  • Managing trader’s escalations to resolution, freeing up user’s time to focus on their core responsibility, leading triage L2 Desktop Support incidents assigned from L1 Service Desk
  • Acting as a POC for the business with IT related issues, performing root cause analysis, documenting the resolution, updating KB articles
  • Meeting or exceeding Service Level Agreements, providing a permanent resolution or workaround, undertaking tasks and project work as directed by the business, application owners, and line manager

What we’ re looking for:

  • Minimum four years' experience supporting Front Office Investment Banking Businesses, troubleshooting Windows, Apple, BYOD, and Market Data applications
  • Excellent desktop support experience, customer forward, with great interpersonal and communication skills
  • Solid technical skills, with a demonstrated ability to learn, identify, and resolve systemic problems
  • Ability to triage and work individually and on a team in a fast-paced environment


Skills that will help you in the role:

  • Bachelor's degree in IT, or equivalent work experience
  • Foundational understanding of ITIL concepts and Helpdesk best practices
  • Proficiency in English and Mandarin
  • Ability to triage and work individually and on a team in a fast-paced environment
