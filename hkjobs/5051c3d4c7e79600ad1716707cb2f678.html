Position Overview

The firm is seeking a seasoned sales professional with excellent integrity, drive and commitment, as part of the growth of J.P. Morgan Asset Management (JPMAM) ’s Private Bank Distribution Business in Asia.

This is a highly visible role and requires an individual who can partner closely with both clients and colleagues globally to deliver our strategic priorities.

Specific responsibilities include:

  • Build and deepen strong business partnerships for the growth of sales and assets, as well as the retention of assets, of J.P. Morgan Asset Management’s investment products and solutions across the Private Bank channel in Asia
  • Develop and maintain relationships with key decision makers, gatekeepers and centers of influence; execute funds onboarding & maximize fundraising on client’s platform
  • Understand and analyze industry development and translate into business opportunity
  • Prepare business report and handle business agreement related issues
  • Work effectively with different internal parties and able to present on the team's behalf
  • As a team to achieve overall business objectives, i.e. sales targets and other KPIs

Core Skills & Qualifications required

The successful candidate will have an established track record, with at least 5 years of experience in financial industry, developing and maintaining strong relationships with key clients.

They should also be able to demonstrate the following core skills:

  • Technical Expertise – deep understanding of the funds and private banking industry, clients and competition landscape; extensive market and product knowledge
  • Drive for Achievement – proven track record of in a financial sales environment; outstanding energy and results-oriented
  • People and Relationship Management – build and manage relationships across organization and with clients
  • Strong fiduciary and risk & control mindsets, with high standard of integrity
  • Excellent communications in both English and Cantonese
