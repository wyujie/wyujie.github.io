J.P. Morgan - Vice President, Insurance Coordinator, Private Banking


2023-02-11 - Product - Hong Kong

Key Responsibilities

The role will manage the (a) day-to-day / insurance referral activities and governance for J.P. Morgan Asia Private Bank by partnering closely with client advisors and enabling functions covering the following:

 

  • accountable for insurance referral activities related policies and procedures and interface with internal teams to resolve operational issues;
  • support client advisors in end-to-end  insurance referral activities;
  • responsible for the engagement model, broker referral agreements and relationships with insurance brokers;
  • manage appropriate risk and control governance framework for insurance referral activities;
  • provide and coordinate internal insurance referral related communication and trainings; and
  • drive insurance referral and wealth planning related projects.

 

The Ideal Candidate

  • 8+ years of experience in life insurance and wealth management at a private bank or life insurance broker in Asia
  • Highly disciplined individual, self-motivated, fast learner, performs well under pressure in dynamic environment
  • Extremely organized and delivery focused with the ability to work independently and balance multiple priorities, while remaining focused on accuracy and attention to detail. Ability to deliver solutions to a tight deadline regardless of complexity and to escalate issues as needed
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills with the ability to work in a team environment, with people at all levels across all functions including ability to engage with senior management
  • Strong problem-solving skill. Ability to break down complex problems into components and find solutions to a wide variety of compliance, legal, technological, and behavioural constraints
  • Fluency in English is a must. Proficiency in Mandarin, Cantonese and other Asian languages would be a bonus
