JPMorgan Chase & Co. (NYSE: JPM) is a leading global financial services firm with assets of $3 trillion and operations in more than 60 countries. The firm is a leader in investment banking, financial services for consumers, small business and commercial banking, financial transaction processing and asset management.

The APAC Chief Data Office (CDO) is a business-centric organization whose purpose is to unlock value for our stakeholders through timely delivery of high-quality, compliant data. The CDO oversees the overall strategy and execution of the Business’ data agenda.  The Strategic Execution team is responsible for oversight of the program/project planning and execution; policy/standard requirements implementation planning and execution; facilitating the assessment of new tactical and strategic demands impacting data governance.

The team works closely with the CIB data pillar teams (Data Use; Data Quality; Data Storage; Data Protection; Retention & Destruction; Data Governance) and Control Management to maintain project standards and partner on implementation of data requirements. The role calls for a candidate to support multiple initiatives while partnering across global teams and regularly collaborating throughout the organization. Additionally, this role will have the opportunity to support the CDO People Agenda and the broader CIB Chief Data and Analytics Office (CDAO) Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) Program.

 Typical responsibilities include:

  • Partner closely with senior Data Product and Data Analytics leads to manage their businesses and drive day-to-day execution including the build and commercialization of new data products and AI/ML solutions, which may include identifying and refining business cases, assessing commerciality, obtaining required approvals, overseeing development and driving marketing plans
  • Drive results through leadership and partnership, managing multiple work streams and priorities concurrently
  • Drive our strategic vision of enabling data use while protecting data as an asset to the firm and help promote this culture within the business
  • Execute project management such as tracking deliverables from identification to completion, preparing materials, coordinating with subject matter experts, and supporting stakeholder meetings
  • Manage short to medium term projects inclusive of project plan development, status tracking, production of project reporting and communications
  • Facilitate the resolution of key issues across stakeholders and produce the related reporting (e.g., risks; actions; issues; dependencies/ decision; path to green) and impact communications
  • Help foster and sustain a global culture that celebrates diversity, equity, and inclusion through participation in the CDAO DEI Council and support for the CDO People Agenda.
