UBS - Program Manager


2023-01-28 - Process, project and program management - Hong Kong

Your role

Are you an experience program manager with a track record of delivering process and strategic change? Do you enjoy delivering enhanced change capabilities across a range of business functions? Do you want to play a key role in the driving our digital transformation in GWM?

We’re looking for a Program Manager to:

• Manage all aspects of a program, which includes project planning, execution, timing, quality, cost and early identification of potential issues.

• Manage a team of experienced Project Managers, either directly or as part of a matrix organization, ensuring efficient use of resources.

• Identify and manage senior stakeholders and clients, evaluate their interests and establish effective communication channels ensuring effective stakeholder management and engagement.

• Maintain a strong project governance structure and process to ensure accurate and timely reporting as well as effective and efficient delivery of the assigned program or sub-program

• support cultural change towards a continuous improvement mind-set

Your team

You'll be working within the Global Wealth Management North Asia Operating Head team in Hong Kong. The function is responsible for driving GWM's digital transformation and operationalizing our strategy to extend our leadership in the region.

Your expertise

• ideally at least ten years line or project management experience
• Outstanding leadership and organizational skills
• process optimization experience (Lean or Six Sigma knowledge and skills are a plus)
• agile methodology experience
• strong analytical, communication and facilitation (workshops) skills
• able to collaborate with all levels within the organization especially senior stakeholders
• experienced in change programs
