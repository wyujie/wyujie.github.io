Macquarie - Financial Management Group - Graduate Hong Kong Central team


2023-02-14 - - Hong Kong

Macquarie Group is a global diversified financial group providing clients with asset management and finance, banking, advisory, risk and capital solutions across debt, equity and commodities. 

Founded in 1969, Macquarie employs more than 13,500 people in 27 countries.  Do you want to experience what it is like to be part of a talented global team? Are you instinctively entrepreneurial and ambitious in nature? If you’re determined to succeed we can help you reach your potential.

At Macquarie, we look for innovative, driven and talented students who share our drive for excellence.

The Financial Management Group (FMG) of Macquarie provides finance and accounting services to all areas of the business. We are currently looking for a graduate to work in the Financial Management Group in Macquarie’s Hong Kong office. This is a full-time position and we are looking for graduates to join us upon graduation.

The responsibilities will include supporting finance processes in financial controls, regulatory reporting as well as exposures to work on finance process projects and change initiatives. 

This will include supporting with the month-end closing, regulatory return preparation, prepare materials for project communications and other regional communication updates. 

This role will mainly be working with FMG teams across Asia.

You’ll be learning on the job, working on daily regulatory calculations, completing financial and regulatory reporting month end with tight timelines.

The experience you will have throughout your program can set you up for a successful career following the completion of your studies.

We are looking for individuals with an Accounting or Business Administration background. The individual is required to have strong communication skills, high attention to detail and good computer literacy skills in Microsoft Excels.  Other systems that will be used are Oracle Fusion GL, Microsoft PowerPoint and word.  The candidate needs to be very comfortable working with numbers and data.  Fluency in English is required and knowing other Asian languages would be advantage as we deal with many locations throughout Asia.

About the Financial Management Group

In our Financial Management Group you will be part of a team that provides strategic analysis and advice on capital, funding, liquidity, tax and financial performance to support the growth of Macquarie's businesses around the world. You will join a team that ensures Macquarie continues to meet its financial, regulatory and tax reporting compliance obligations in the jurisdictions in which we operate, and a team that maintains significant relationships on behalf of the Group.
