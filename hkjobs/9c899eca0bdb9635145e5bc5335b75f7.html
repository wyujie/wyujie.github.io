Morgan Stanley - Asia HR Business Management & Communications Associate


2023-02-14 - Human Resources - Hong Kong, Hong Kong, Hong Kong

About The Organization 


Morgan Stanley is one of the world’s largest diversified financial services companies, with a reputation for excellence in advice and execution on a global scale. The Firm’s 60,000+ employees serve clients worldwide including corporations, governments, institutions and individuals from more than 700+ offices in 40 countries.


Since its founding in 1935, Morgan Stanley and its people have helped redefine the meaning of financial services. The firm has continually broken new ground in advising its clients on strategic transactions, in pioneering the global expansion of finance and capital markets, and in providing new opportunities for individual and institutional investors. 


About The Role


This role is a unique opportunity to become part of a team of strategic Human Resource professionals. The HR Management team is at the center of the HR department and manages the day to day needs of HR to ensure we meet our strategic goals, are innovating as a function and are prudent around our finances and our operating efficiency. 
The successful candidate will have the opportunity to get involved in HR budgeting and financial management, act as a junior HR Business Partner for the HR team (300+ people across the region) and manage the communications within the department. They will be supported by an experienced HR management team and will gradually take on responsibility for selected deliverables and projects within HR.


Responsibilities:


HR Business Management:


o    Help to drive innovation and efficiency by supporting the management of the HR Management Budgets, Forecasts and Actual Spend Tracking Process
o    Manage contingent, travel & entertainment spend across the region
o    Be a champion of change, share best practices and support the team in adopting new practices and initiatives, including new HR systems/processes/ data analytics where appropriate
o    Support projects across the broader HR Management function 
o    Support risk management requirements for the department


HR Communications:


o    Draft monthly HR newsletter and other HR communications
o    Manage the HR internal site and review content on a regular basis
o    Oversee the communications calendar to ensure proper coordination across teams
o    Review and update employee guides
o    Organize training and other department wide events


HR for HR:


o    Ensure accurate data of new hires/employees in Workday system - update reporting line, cost center changes for employees, leave tracking, one-time payment initiation, transfer records, etc.
o    Responsible for end-to-end job data change management
o    Provide support for the hiring, onboarding and offboarding of employees
o    Run headcount and other HR reports and liaise with the global team for headcount requests
o    Responsible for contingent workforce lifecycle management
 

Qualifications / Skills Requirements:


•    University degree in Human Resources or related field
•    3+ years of experience in the HR field with a MNC background
•    Demonstrated ability in Microsoft Office (Outlook, PowerPoint, Excel, Word). Advanced Excel skills preferred
•    Strong attention to detail and accuracy
•    Superior time management and organization skills
•    Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English 
•    Problem-solver and capable of multi-tasking to meet deadlines in a demanding environment 
•    Build relationships with colleagues locally and globally both within and outside of HR, sharing ideas and information
•    Liaise with global partners to support the business
•    Able to work in a fast-paced, high change environment
