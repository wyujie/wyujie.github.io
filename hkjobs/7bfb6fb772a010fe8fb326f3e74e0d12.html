Jane Street - Events Coordinator, Creative Engagement


2023-01-16 - Creative - Hong Kong

About the Position

We are looking for an Events Coordinator to join our Creative Engagement team who can help expand the events offered in our Asia offices. The right person will have a service mentality and enjoy building relationships with all sorts of teams across our firm to understand their team goals and what types of events would best suit them.

Our employees are key to our firm's success, and we work hard to find opportunities for individuals to connect with our culture and each other. We aim to offer events that support the broader goals of the firm, while also keeping our colleagues feeling engaged, supported, and excited to come to work. 

We also use our event offerings to shape the way people outside of Jane Street interact with our firm, including students and professionals who are looking to work with us.

You will be indispensable to both pieces of this effort — designing, planning, and executing events for both current employees and college recruits in our Asia offices. Doing so will require collaborating with our Recruiting, Human Resources, and Office Administration teams. 

Additional responsibilities of this role will include:

  • Building relationships across the firm to understand our culture and employees
  • Organizing events from start to finish, including but not limited to venue selection, catering, entertainment, transportation, promotion, RSVP management, and on-site event-day support
  • Maintaining a database of all event details and costs
  • Working with our Legal team on all event-related contracts

About You

  • Bachelor’s degree and experience in event management
  • Intellectually curious, creative, and eager to learn
  • Reliable and flexible team player with a positive attitude 
  • Willing to work with a flexible schedule that may include some early mornings, evening hours, and after-hours events
  • Strong people skills and a customer service mentality; can prioritize and resolve varying and sometimes conflicting requests
  • Excellent communication skills, both spoken and written
  • Fluency in English and Cantonese required
