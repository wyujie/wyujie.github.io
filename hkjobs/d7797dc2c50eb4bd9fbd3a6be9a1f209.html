Group Audit – Principal Auditor – Vice President

Location: Hong Kong

Position Overview

Details of the Division and Team:

Reporting to a Principal Audit Manager, the Principal Auditor will work as part of a multi-disciplinary audit team within Hong Kong.  Whilst the work will predominantly focus on auditing the Investment Bank Origination & Advisory Division (Corporate Finance) there will also be the need to cover other areas of the bank from time to time.  Based in Hong Kong, the role will allow for limited travel to other DB locations, mainly within Asia Pacific.

What we will offer you:

A healthy, engaged and well-supported workforce are better equipped to do their best work and, more importantly, enjoy their lives inside and outside the workplace. That’s why we are committed to providing an environment with your development and wellbeing at its center.
 

You can expect:

  • Competitive Salary and non-contributory pension

  • 20 days annual leave plus generous number of public holidays

  • Life Assurance and Private Healthcare for you and your family

  • A range of flexible benefits including retail discounts, gym benefits, wellbeing incentives

  • The opportunity to support a wide ranging CSR program plus take volunteering leave days

Your key responsibilities:

  • Execute day-to-day operational audit work and contribute to the delivery of audits (including the Continuous Monitoring process and the updating of Risk Assessment Profiles).

  • Lead audit assignments, drafts audit findings / reports for review by audit management, facilitate issue tracking and validate closure of issue.

  • Lead and execute audit work in line with the agreed audit approach e.g. documenting Activity / Process Flows, identification of key risks and testing of key controls to determine whether they are properly designed and are operating effectively and documenting work in accordance with Group Audit (GA) standards.

  • Complete all assigned work in line with agreed budgets, including ad hoc projects and special investigations.

  • Proactively develop and maintain professional working relationships with colleagues, the business and respective support areas.

  • Partner with other audit teams (regionally and / or globally) such as Risk, Finance, Technology and Operations during audit engagements to ensure comprehensive coverage and an integrated approach to front to back audits of Investment Bank Origination & Advisory and its associated support functions.

  • Communicate openly with GA management and the internal stakeholders; keep them informed of potential issues and escalate problems / delays accordingly.

  • Keep abreast of pertinent industry, regulatory and business practices.

  • Deliver and sustain own performance against key objectives in the context of functional strategies.

  • Support the development of an environment where people management and development is the number one priority.

  • Take ownership for own career management, seek opportunities for continuous development of personal capability and improve performance contribution.

Your skills and experience:

  • Knowledge and experience of financial services particularly Investment Banking products including interest rate, foreign exchange, equity, and structured products, coupled with familiarization of working within the operational requirements and regulatory environment of a global Bank. 

  • Professional credentials, including degree level education and a relevant professional qualification.

  • Knowledge of auditing standards and concepts.

  • Fluency in English and Chinese (written and verbal) languages as this role requires liaising with stakeholders in Taiwan & China

  • A proven track record of meeting client needs in a financial services environment

  • Excellent team working capabilities and an ability to build strong business and team relationships.

  • Strong strategic / analytical thinking.

  • Strong project management and planning skills.

  • Able to convey, complex concepts and ideas on issues requiring interpretation and opinion, in a logical and structured approach.

  • Able to challenge and be challenged whilst maintaining the highest levels of professionalism.

  • Experienced in delivering work under tight deadlines.

Role is required to be performed primarily on-site at the International Commerce Centre.  Relevant health and safety requirements apply.
