J.P. Morgan - Corporate & Investment Bank - Equities Market Risk - Executive Director


2023-02-01 - Market Risk - Central and Western, Hong Kong Island, Hong Kong

As part of Risk Management and Compliance, you are at the centre of keeping JPMorgan Chase strong and resilient.  Market Risk is seeking an Executive Director to lead the Equities Market Risk Coverage Team in Hong Kong.  Not only will you be responsible for Equities Market Risk in the region you will also be the key point of contact to the Hong Kong CRO, across market risk in the entity.

Job Description

As part of Risk Management and Compliance, you are at the centre of keeping JPMorgan Chase strong and resilient. You help the firm grow its business in a responsible way by anticipating new and emerging risks and using your expert judgement to solve real-world challenges that impact our company, customers and communities.  Our culture in Risk Management and Compliance is all about thinking outside the box, challenging the status quo and striving to be best-in-class.

As a part of the CIB Market Risk Management group, you will be working in a cutting-edge Risk Organization.  As a seasoned Market Risk Manager we look to you to identify, monitor and measure the risks the business is exposed to. You and your team will look for innovative ways to identify threats and weaknesses in the risk profile and to challenge and escalate as you see appropriate.  As part of the Equities Market Risk Coverage team you will be working alongside one of the largest Equities businesses in the street and get to use your intellectual curiosity and natural interest in global financial markets: focusing on price movements and events affecting the trading portfolio, with the goal of highlighting top risks with the Trading business and the Market Risk management team.The suite of products you will risk manage is broad, the ideal candidate will have an extensive product knowledge and thorough understanding of risk managing equity derivatives and options.The role reports to the head of Market Risk Coverage for Europe, Middle East, Africa, and Asia Pacific Equities.

Responsibilities include:

  • You & your team, based in Hong Kong, Shanghai and India, will perform daily monitoring and analysis of risk exposures and understanding the factors that drive the profits and loss affecting the trading portfolio
  • Key decision maker on the relevant trading limits and pre-trade approvals
  • At the same time you will know when to escalate, working within the boundaries of your experience
  • You will benefit from interacting regularly with traders both in the region and globally and will be experienced in challenging the business around risk decisions
  • Identify top risks and communicate those risks to the trading business and the Market Risk management team
  • Develop a deep understanding of the wide-ranging suite of financial products traded by JP Morgan and the key risks associated with them
  • Perform & understand the output from scenario analysis and stress testing;  be able to articulate those results, as well as findings from ad-hoc regular risk analysis, into meaningful executive summaries
  • Dealing with local regulatory authorities directly on Market Risk related enquiries and inspections
  • Lead the roll-out of the firm’s Hong Kong Climate Stress framework

Minimum Skills, Experience and Qualifications

We are looking for someone excited to join our organization. If you meet the minimum requirements below, you are encouraged to apply to be considered for this role.

  • Previous work experience in the financial industry, preferably in Trading, Risk Management or Regulatory experience
  • Prior experience managing teams is preferred.
  • Familiarity with market risk strongly preferred
  • Strong quantitative and analytical background with extensive product knowledge and thorough understanding of risk managing equity derivatives and options
  • Experience of using technology to drive improvement would be a great asset
  • An ability to take data and transform it in to meaningful executive information
  • Strong stakeholder management, a history of working across different businesses, cultures and personalities
