Position Overview

Details of the Division and Team:

The Custody Settlement group is responsible for managing and supporting all settlement related processes which occur on SD-1 and SD. 

The primary function of the team is to mitigate either reputational or financial risk that can occur through processing and is achieved through the primary responsibilities listed below.

What we will offer you:

A healthy, engaged and well-supported workforce is better equipped to do their best work and, more importantly, enjoy their lives inside and outside the workplace. That’s why we are committed to providing an environment with your development and wellbeing at its center.

  • Competitive Salary and non-contributory pension                                       

  • 20 days annual leave plus generous number of public holidays

  • A range of flexible benefits including retail discounts, gym benefits, wellbeing incentives                                                                          

  • The opportunity to support a wide ranging CSR program plus take volunteering leave days

Your key responsibilities:

  • Handle settlement/Corporation  for HK and Overseas Equities, Bonds  

  • Client instructions preparation in in-house system

  • Pre-matching with counterparty

  • CCASS and ECMT terminal handling

  • Client reporting for pending, failed and settled trades

  • Prepare voucher for cash posting

  • Monitor the performance of the Line against the pre-agreed Service Level Documents that exist between each processing function.

  • Monitor the Nostro and depot breaks ensure valid narration, completeness and progress.

  • Monitor the high value nostros ensuring completeness and validity of narration.

  • Exception processing

  • System upgrade testing prior to implementation

  • Handle Stamp Duty Payment

  • Handle Physical Certificate conversion

  • Handle Cash Payment

Your skills and experience:

  • Degree holder preferred and with at least 5 years of relevant experience

  • Familiarize with HK and Overseas Equities settlement/Corporation in custodian business.

  • Good command of written & spoken English and Chinese

  • Proficient in Excel, Word and fast typing both in English & Chinese

  • Able to work independently and under pressure

  • Able to identify risks and loopholes in operations and escalate to management

  • Able to provide recommendation to management to streamline processing or minimize operation risks

  • The ability to remotely manage.

  • The candidate must be highly motivated, highly efficient and have the ability to constantly deliver on time.

  • The candidate must be willing to take on any role within the team at any time.


Role is required to be performed primarily on-site at the International Commerce Centre.  Relevant health and safety requirements apply.
