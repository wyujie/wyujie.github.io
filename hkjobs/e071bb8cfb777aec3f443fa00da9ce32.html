J.P. Morgan - CIB Operations - DCM MTN and ISDA Documentation Specialist - Associate


2023-01-24 - Reference Data - Hong Kong

Digital Document Services (DDS) is part of Corporate & Investment Bank (CIB) Operations that supports documentation management functions for businesses throughout the CIB. To adhere to the Documentation Management Policy, DDS is implementing a common global operating model for the end-to-end lifecycle of client documentation across CIB inclusive of people, process, and technology while continuing to support our business partners and meet regulatory requirements in a controlled manner. 

Role Description

  • Working closely with regional Business/Legal/Trading Department and other stakeholders to drive and manage the documentation process of medium-term notes (MTN) (mainly, MTN program updates and drawdowns) and derivatives transactions.
  • Review MTN program update documents (such as Program Agreements, Due Diligence Questionnaires), identify issues and co-ordinate with the relevant stakeholders for prompt resolution on the issues within the set timeframe.
  • Obtain approvals/waivers/sign-off from relevant stakeholders on any concessions to standard policy/approach during the documentation process as needed.
  • Draft post-trade documents (mainly, Pricing Supplement) for MTN drawdowns, liaise with stakeholders and drive the documentation process to the completion.
  • Perform documentation management functions including document execution, capture, maintenance, and control with DDS strategic tools, ensuring correct translation of legal documentation into the systems for data capture.
  • Become a point of contact for Business/Legal (and other stakeholders) to facilitate the master agreement onboarding process (mainly ISDA/CSA/GMRA/GMSLA/MTN program documentation) - from the inception to execution and system set-up.
  • Track full populations of document negotiations throughout the process and hold regular meetings with stakeholders for governance and transparency.
  • Resolve document/system queries with partners in Business/Legal/Risk/Operations/Technology under an efficient operating framework.
  • Explore the opportunities of automation and process enhancement by partnering with Legal, Business, Operations and Technology.


Qualifications/Experience/Knowledge/Skills

  • Minimum of 1 - 2 years proven experience in the area of debt capital markets gained from major financial institution or law firm environment.
  • Law degree an advantage.
  • An excellent understanding of the MTN program documents/updates/drawdowns mechanics preferred.
  • Proven track record of managing a wide range of client documents/negotiations with good understanding of APAC regional issues.
  • Experience of derivatives ISDA documentation an advantage.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills of spoken and written English. Mandarin Chinese or Korean an advantage.
  • Ability to perform/prioritize multi-tasks and excellent execution mentality to meet deadlines.
  • Ability to work and deliver independently/proactively.
  • Strong organizational and teamwork skills.
  • Keep abreast of regulatory reforms and innovation of tools/process/automation.
  • Good skills of MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint). Data management/analysis skills an advantage.
