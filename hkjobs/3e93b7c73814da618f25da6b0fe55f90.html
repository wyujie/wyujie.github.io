Business Support Management (BSM) is a function within CIB Finance & Business Management.  The group is responsible for administrative and control-focused support to our CIB businesses. This includes, but is not limited to, oversight and management of Administrative Assistants and general support of day-to-day business functions and controls.

The team interacts heavily with multiple lines of business, Business Managers, CIB BSM global counterparts, and Internal Business Partners such as Business Control, Compliance, Human Resources, and Technology.

Responsibilities:

  • Management of on/off boarding process (verification checks, provision of technology including hardware, applications, market data)
  • Implementation of controls including consecutive leave, physical access management, business resiliency support, centralized policy & procedure
  • Real Estate coordination: space planning, move management, location strategy
  • Represent CIB in firmwide and regional real estate strategy reviews and execution of associated logistics
  • Communicate and coordinate implementation of firmwide policies and changes in strategy within CIB
  • Liaison with Global Real Estate and Facilities on projects and day to running of our locations
  • Ensuring accuracy of headcount projections used in space planning (HLP)
  • Reporting and analytics on our headcount and real estate footprint
  • Assist in day-to-day issues that arise (seating constraints, Tririga/data issues, etc.)
  • Collaborate with people across all levels in CIB to execute deliverables.
  • Partner with managers and HR to manage technology accesses for joiners and leavers.
  • Handle ad-hoc requests from business related to technology, real estate, resource allocation, etc.
  • Analysis of operational changes and asses the change impact.
  • Analyze and summarize data for presentation and interpretation.
  • Manage space assignments to optimize the utilization.
  • Respond to audit and compliance queries.
  • Plan and oversee ad-hoc projects from execution thru completion.
  • Partnership with regional and global teams to ensure business initiatives are achieve.

Requirements:

  • Minimum of a Bachelor's Degree
  • Minimum of 3 years of experience preferably in Office Management or Business Management in an Investment Banking environment
  • A team player, operates independently, has exceptional judgment and organizational ability, highly effective problem-solving skills
  • Pro-active in taking the initiative demonstrating strong leadership, a self- starter with a proactive approach to problem solving and implementing improvements.
  • Ability to thrive on challenges in an unstructured working environment.
  • Effective communicator, with the ability to communicate in a clear and concise manner. Create logical and conclusive presentations highlighting key issues, summaries, and briefs for stakeholder management at various levels of seniority
  • Strong delivery focus with an action oriented mind-set and willingness to take responsibility.
  • Ability to drive into the detail of process, technical and organizational issues whilst maintaining an appreciation for a broader strategy
  • Ability to conduct analytical reviews to influence and drive decision making
  • Strong control mind-set and attention to detail with the ability to execute operational process
  • Awareness of J.P. Morgan key lines of business and organizational structure
  • Develop relationships and demonstrate influence across a range of functions and nurturing a large network
  • Proficiency in both written and spoken English and preferably Chinese
