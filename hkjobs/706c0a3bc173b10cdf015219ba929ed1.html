We are currently seeking a high calibre professional to join our team as a Technical Specialist .

Principal Responsibilities

  • Work within the Workspace fulfilment pod, covering some or all: Agile way of working for Windows and Apple Desktop Infrastructure related products, 3rd level support of said products, thought technical documentation, verbal and written communication
  • Be part of a team that performs sustainable continuous improvement, identifying and removing organisational barriers affecting the team, processes and products owned
  • Maintain an agile mind-set by adopting the practices and processes identified as being required for our new pods, leveraging agile delivery methods, associated frameworks (e.g., Lean) and Bank tools (e.g., JIRA) to do this
  • Understand and follow the ITID strategy and vision and the Product Management direction
  • Identify continuous improvement opportunities within the team, focusing on automation, process improvement, how technology is best leveraged and technology shift opportunities
  • Work collaboratively with staff within the platform to identify and agree improvement opportunities
Requirements

Requirements

  • At least 3-5 years of experience in the Desktop environment, with solid experience in Windows related technologies. Experience in Apple Mac and linked technologies, Citrix and Virtualisation services will be a plus
  • Experience working in large-scale multinational companies
  • Experience of working as part of an Operations OR agile team is a plus
  • Interpersonal skills – demonstrate good judgement and a good level of communication. Ability to articulate complex technical issues concisely
  • Knowledge of technical components of the Windows environments, Windows OS, Windows Controls and policies, Apple Mac and linked technologies, Citrix and Virtualisation services
  • Experience of associated technology areas: Microsoft Office, browsers (Safari, IE, Chrome), Java, security models and tooling, identity and access management tooling, Active Directory, Workspace One, Apple Remote Desktop, Active Directory, SCCM, Azure
  • Work independently and under high pressure, including but not limited to meet tight deadline and incident management
  • Scripting and automation experiences, with proficiency in the use of Power Shell scripting is a plus
  • Knowledge and experience of using agile delivery methods and tools to implement both tactical and strategic changes is a plus
  • Respectful of different cultures, working with colleagues from across all the globe
