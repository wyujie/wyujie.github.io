Barclays - APAC Delta1 BAPM


2023-02-12 - Technology - Hong Kong, Hong Kong China

APAC Equity AVP
Hong Kong
As a Barclays APAC Equity AVP, you will be responsible for overseeing the business analysis function for APAC Equity Index trading technology, covering all projects in the space including Growth, MRC (Mandatory, Reg & Control) and KTLO (Keep The Lights On). You will be the key SME (Subject Matter Expert) within Technology across Equity Index trading platforms, interfacing with Trading desks, Market Risk, Compliance, Middle Office and front to back Technology teams.
Barclays is one of the world's largest and most respected financial institutions, with 329 years of success, quality and innovation behind us. We offer careers that provide endless opportunity – helping millions of individuals and businesses thrive, and creating financial and digital solutions that the world now takes for granted.

What will you be doing?

  • Overseeing of the APAC Equity index solution book of work and delivery/financial planning status across regional Prime Delta One projects
  • Partnering with Tech leads to drive application story backlogs/sprint planning and provide robust framework to ensure dev/QA are able to complete delivery of their book of work
  • Driving a standardised and structured approach to project delivery across Equity Prime Delta One projects
  • Driving process improvements and ensure consistent business analysis task structure in place across Equity Prime Delta One project deliveries.
  • Owning setup and maintenance of central repository for requirements for Equity Prime Delta One (Confluence, JIRA)
  • Being responsible for assessment of any new cross team/FTB requirement requests across Equity Prime Delta One
  • Ensuring all APAC Equity Prime Delta One trading flows are documented in detail and kept up to date as new flows are rolled out/decommissioned.
  • Ensuring compliance with internal and external controls/regulatory policies including GET policy

What we’re looking for:

  • First-hand experience working on a front-office trading platform
  • Ability to write high quality Business Requirement Documents and Functional Specs that are clear and concise enough for both traders and developers to follow
  • Ability to build robust relationships with the Front Office, Operations, Compliance and Technology
  • In-depth understanding of front to back Equity trading flows and processes

Skills that will help you in the role:

  • Technical background desirable
  • Good understand of east Asia market micro-structure
  • Experience on market making, index arbitrage trading platform
  • Posses the drive to apply order and process, yet flexible to adapt as needed for continuous improvement

Where will you be working?
Our offices are situated in the center of Hong Kong’s financial district, at 2 Queen’s Road.
Cheung Kong Centre is an investment banking hub that offers panoramic views of Victoria Harbour and Kowloon Peninsula. There is a variety of local amenities and you will be just a short walk from shopping malls, health clubs and multiple transport links.
