We are currently seeking a high calibre professional to join our team as a Sanctions Advisory Manager.

Principal Responsibilities

This position is a manager position in the Regional Sanctions Advisory Team in providing key support to the Region, Country teams and Global Business Line Head of Sanctions with Sanctions escalations. The role holder is responsible for the second line of defence oversight of sanctions risk within the Region through the application of the activities within the Regional Sanctions Advisory team.

The role holder will be responsible for the provision of Regional subject matter expertise for all Sanctions matters including Customer and Transaction approvals, dispensations, licences and potential breaches across the Region. The role holder will be required to provide advice and recommendations for specific Sanctions risks, working closely with Business Lines, and Country Sanctions teams to ensure advice is supported by specific guidance and procedure documents.

The role holder will be required to understand and keep up to date with the Group strategy on Sanctions advisory process and the impact that is has on the Region. The role holder will need to ensure all Sanctions advisory process are adhered to the requirements as set out in the Global Sanctions Policy and relevant Sanctions procedures. The nature of the role requires close working contact with all teams across the Region

  • Identifying and challenging any potential Sanctions risk inherent in Global Business Line decisions and client activity
  • Participating in regional level oversight of all Advisory issues and provide support to Global Business Lines, Product and Countries
  • Providing Advisory input into the completion of the Enterprise Wide Risk Assessment (EWRA), acting as a point of contact for queries and support
  • Acting as the key point of contact within Region for all Sanctions Advisory matters, providing Regional Level Sanctions leadership and subject matter expertise
  • Support and provide subject matter expertise to the implementation of globally led Sanctions change initiatives within Region and more specifically within the Sanctions Advisory Team

  • Experience of dealing with regulatory matters and confident interaction with Regulators
  • Ability to develop practical, cost effective solutions to complex issues
  • High level of English language skills
