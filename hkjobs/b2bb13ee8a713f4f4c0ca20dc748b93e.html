2023-01-16 - Core Infrastructure - Hong Kong

Equity Risk Analyst

Job Function Summary

Millennium’s Equity Risk Management Department is responsible for identifying, measuring, monitoring, managing, and reporting on the risks associated with Millennium portfolios. Our Risk Management organization is designed to accommodate the overall size, nature, and complexity of the firm’s trading activities.

Equity Risk in Asia is seeking a Risk Analyst to join the team.  The ideal candidate would have had exposure building and running risk models, stress analytical tools, performance attribution, style analysis, or ad hoc analyses. The incumbent will also have the opportunity to work closely with quants to program  and generate performance attributions and risk scenarios.

Principal Responsibilities

  • Develop and support tools for monitoring the risks of Asia focused Equity PMs.
  • Part of the job will involves operational duties of  trading limit active monitoring which is a preferred venue to learn about risk process implementation.
  • Investigate, review and analyze the risk attributes and their evolutions, working with other teams to provide holistic analysis
  • Contribute to longer term projects (including research work into financial markets, new trends etc) and assist in day-to-day tasks

Qualifications/Skills Required

  • Postgraduate with major in quantitative discipline: statistics, maths, physics, engineering, computer science, complemented by a  quantitative finance degree (flexible here : can be undergrad computer science with quant finance master from reputable university)..
  • 3-5 years’ relevant work experience:
  • Excellent communication, problem-solving and analytical skills
  • Strong quantitative background
  • Deep knowledge and demonstrated experiences in Python, R, SQL, Excel, and VBA– large data manipulation via programing analysis (statistical and others)
  • Understanding of how the various capital markets (such as equity and fixed income) work with good knowledge of financial products (such as swap, options)
  • Ability to work independently and complete set tasks within set deadlines
  • Attention to detail and quick learner
  • Has initiative, good team player
