Operations
One of the largest divisions in the Firm, Operations is the first line of defense for Morgan Stanley's financial and reputational well-being and essential in delivering value. Operations is responsible for correctly settling and recording millions of transactions per day, identifying, and mitigating operational risk, developing strong client relationships and partnering with technology to realize the full potential of IT and Automation. Operations has a premier team of creative and dedicated professionals, with a unique vantage point interacting with all parts of the business. The Operations Senior Management team is passionate about our people development and providing top-notch employee opportunities through an inclusive, forward-looking environment defined by open communication, collaboration, learning and dedicated career management.
Team Profile
The ISG BRM Operations and Collateral & Indications Operations are comprised of BRM Operations responsible for Trade Capture, Lifecycle Management, Exposure Management and Collateral Optimization of Securities Financing Transactions in the APAC markets; Collateral Management and Indications, including Margin Processing, Portfolio Reconciliation and Margin Loan support.

Role Description & Responsibilities
> You will be a part of Hong Kong BRM Operations and Collateral & Indications Operations, comprising of Collateral Management and Indications, including Margin Processing; Financing Operations is comprised of Trade Capture, Lifecycle Management, Exposure Management and Collateral Optimization of Securities Financing & Securities Lending Transactions.
> You will report to the manager of Hong Kong BRM Operations and Collateral & Indications Operations and play a key role to perform team's day-to-day functions and supervise a team member.
> Your daily responsibilities include performing multiple operations' functions that support the Collateral Operations, namely, portfolio reconciliation and margin loan processing. Also, as a senior member of the team, you will be a go-to person for internal teams for consultation for new businesses and products.
