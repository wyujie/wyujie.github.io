Macquarie - Delta One Sales


2023-02-14 - - Hong Kong

An exciting opportunity to join our Delta One sales team, as we look to build on an established track record of success across the Asia region.

As a member of our Delta One sales team, you will be part of a supportive and high performing team within our Equities division. As a hardworking and passionate individual, you’ll be responsible for identifying, developing and maintaining key client relationships. This will also include selling the platform of the firm to Hedge Funds, Mutual Funds and Corporate clients.

As the successful candidate, you will have a passion for equities and financial markets, and you’ll have experience in a similar role. With a deep understanding of selling and trading synthetic futures, custom baskets, sector baskets, stock borrow, IPO financing, options, long financing, p-notes, outperformance notes and index swaps, you’ll have an emphasis on transactional synthetic execution and an ability to increase percentage of swap revenues with existing cash clients.

You’ll have the ability to communicate ideas succinctly as both your written and verbal communication skills are excellent.  Your institutional client relationships are strong, and you are willing to develop new ones. Building relationships internally will be just as important to your success. A basic knowledge of options, strong public speaking skills, and fluency in Mandarin would also be beneficial.

If you have a flair for sales and are passionate about Equity Markets, apply now.

About Macquarie Capital

Macquarie Capital offers global capability in M&A Advisory, Debt and Equity Capital Markets, Principal Finance, and Equity Trading and Research underpinned by deep specialist expertise across a range of sectors. Our people are empowered to think innovatively to address client challenges and originate opportunities, thinking beyond individual transactions to become a trusted adviser and partner over the long haul. With staff in 35 offices transacting across 31 countries, Macquarie Capital is a truly global business.
