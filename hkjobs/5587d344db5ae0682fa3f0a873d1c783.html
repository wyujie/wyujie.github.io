Macquarie - Equity Sales Support Executive


2023-02-14 - - Hong Kong

Our Equities division in Hong Kong is looking for a driven and enthusiastic professional to support our highly successful sales team in striving for customer success with data analytics.

Our Equities Sales team has enjoyed a long track record of success. To support our business growth, we are looking for an ambitious professional to support across all aspects of the team with a focus on driving client relationships through an integrated quantitative approach, using data analytics to empower customer insight generation, create growth opportunities and enhance our existing product offerings. You will play a leading role in developing our customer data analytics, reporting capabilities, and ensuring our operational excellence. In addition to working across regional research, sales and sales trading teams, you’ll also have the opportunity to interact directly with clients, managing queries and supporting the senior sales staff.

As the successful candidate, you will have passion for financial markets and with experience working in technology consulting, CRM companies (such as Salesforce, SAP) or in roles that rely on your ability in using data analytics to analyse opportunities and connect them. You’ll have a passion for data and have an ability to articulate the story behind the numbers, while being empowered to work with Account Managers and interpret the opportunities derived from the data whether from servicing and/or 3rd party commission data. Knowledge of Excel, Power BI and Python or other coding languages would be highly advantageous. You’ll also have a tertiary degree with admirable academic qualifications, ideally in a quantitative/technology discipline. 

You must have the ability to communicate ideas succinctly; both your written and verbal communication skills will be excellent. You’ll have an ability to quickly learn new products and systems, allowing you to thrive in a challenging and constantly changing environment.

If you want to be part of this successful team, apply now.

About Macquarie Capital

Macquarie Capital offers global capability in M&A Advisory, Debt and Equity Capital Markets, Principal Finance, and Equity Trading and Research underpinned by deep specialist expertise across a range of sectors. Our people are empowered to think innovatively to address client challenges and originate opportunities, thinking beyond individual transactions to become a trusted adviser and partner over the long haul. From origination to execution, our unique combination of ideas, capital and expertise is transforming opportunity into reality for our clients and partners around the world.

With staff in 35 offices transacting across 31 countries, Macquarie Capital is a truly global business. A role in the team will expose you to a dynamic working environment and the opportunity to contribute to high-profile transactions whilst working in a business with the best people in the industry.
