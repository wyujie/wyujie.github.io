J.P. Morgan - Financial Analyst, Global Real Estate Asia Pacific – Associate


2023-01-24 - Financial Analysis - Kwun Tong, Kowloon, Hong Kong

The role will report to the Global Real Estate (GRE) APAC Planning Reporting & Analysis lead. Responsibilities include financial reporting, planning, forecasting and analysis supporting J.P. Morgan’s corporate office portfolio and real estate in the Asia Pacific Region.

Specific duties include:

  • Handles monthly reporting, rolling forecast, budgeting cycles and all related analysis on J.P Morgan's self-occupied real estate operating expenses
  • Maintain and track capital expenditure required for office strategies e.g. relocation, consolidation, renovation and aligning P&L to forecast / budget
  • Partner with GRE functional / country heads to track and identify expense reduction opportunities
  • Preparation and presentation of consolidated financial reports
  • Handle and support monthly intercompany billing of real estate expenses allocation to all Line of Business in the region and related reporting, forecasting and budgeting per Corporate calendar
  • Maintain strong Line of Business partnership by providing allocation transparency, analysis and addressing queries
  • Provide recommendations to enhance financial and operational performance of the functions
  • Report on key financial and operational metrics to support business decisions
  • Establishment of strong proactive relationships with all functions
  • Handle ad hoc requests, projects and initiatives
  • Meet all U.S. and regional reporting requirements

Qualifications: 

  • Minimum of a Bachelor’s degree
  • CPA qualification an advantage
  • Minimum 5 years of finance experience in the financial services industry
  • Real estate accounting, tax and transaction experience an advantage
  • Strong analytical and presentation skills
  • Experience in handling and analysing huge databases
  • Ability to work independently and well under pressure, handling multiple projects and initiatives at the same time
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office (PowerPoint, Excel)
