About the Team

The Discretionary Global Macro trading desk is currently a 6-member team split between New York, London and Hong Kong. The primary focus of the team is best-execution cross asset and timely and accurate trade booking in support of all 45 macro pods Point72 has globally covering Interest Rates, FX, Commodities, Credit and Equities. The team works directly with our PMs and Analysts in each pod sourcing liquidity, providing relevant market commentary and is also a liaison with various internal partners including business, operations, margin, compliance and legal.


Responsibilities

  • Work with the teams senior trader in Hong Kong assisting in all aspects a trade lifecycle from pre-trade checks to execution and trade-booking
  • Be responsible for position maintenance (e.g. swap unwinds, future rolls, bilateral novations) and be the point person working with the back office in reconciliation.
  • Develop strong relationships with a broad set of liquidity providers
  • Participate in research projects, including transaction cost analysis and broker/algorithm evaluation
  • Participate in the development of the group’s trading systems

Requirements

  • Ability to exercise sound judgment in assessing and determining how to handle high volumes of queries and issues
  • Keen intuition for markets and a passion for studying them
  • Team player, driven and collaborative
  • Understanding of order lifecycle from order generation to booking & TCA
  • Must exhibit strong problem solving and communication skills
  • We will train talented candidates with little relevant work experience, but evidence of a passion for trading is essential

Preferred

  • 1-2 years of work experience 
  • Familiar with Rates OTC, FX and listed futures markets
