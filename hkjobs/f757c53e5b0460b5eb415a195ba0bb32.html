Role:

 

In carrying out this role the key responsibility is the facilitation and management of the deal lifecycle from mandate through to execution and serving as the first point of contact for Bankers and Trade Sales, providing transaction support (particularly for cross-border and complex transactions), and managing the document execution and deal closing process.

 

The role will report to the Head of Hong Kong Credit Product Delivery. This position will be based in Hong Kong.

 

Responsibilities include:

 

Deal Closing & Client Support

  • Transaction coordination: primary point of contact and escalation across end to end deal lifecycle, including onshore, offshore and cross-border deal coordination
  • Provide insight into negotiation of legal agreements and status of transaction closing.  Escalate backlogs and issues to Bankers as needed to move the transaction along the closing lifecycle
  • Track handoffs,  process and timing/requirements, metrics and escalations as required
  • Pipeline Management and Support
  • Query Resolution

 

Transaction Management

  • Coordination of legal documentation across all lending products
  • Managing the execution of credit facility letters, deal closing process, ownership of sourcing and reviewing client constitutional documents, conditions precedent / subsequent
  • Preparation and coordination of deviations ( including approvals) during negotiations across all categories
  • Populating standard agreements and performing gap analysis / prep work on non standard docs for legal
  • Manage controls framework, reporting and escalation for transaction execution
  • Working across functional areas to ensure all operational, financial and technology risks have been addressed
  • Collaborating with business' and support functions to drive best practices both internally and in the external markets
