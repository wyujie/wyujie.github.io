Equities Full Stack Developer

Hong Kong

As a Barclays Equities Full Stack Developer, Equities are a leading franchise, offering a full-service cash and derivatives business with world class fundamental and macro research and award-winning analytics.   • Developing Java/C# projects defined by business requirements to meet the needs of our clients/desks to provide high-performance, stable, and feature rich trading services
  • Working closely with different teams including business, global development team, regional support/QA team to make sure we deliver the right things in a timely manner
  • Interacting with both senior Front Office Trading and Sales stakeholders at the Director and Managing Director level across multiple regions
  • Effectively understanding requirements from stakeholders

  • Interacting with senior IT based Director and Managing Director level leadership functions for discussions on the project, the product, ongoing work

What we’ re looking for:

  • Five+ years of in-depth JAVA or C# experience, programming skills with good unit testing practices
  • Excellent experience of developing low-latency trading systems and performance tuning techniques on Linux
  • Understanding of higher-level Computer Science concepts such as data structures and algorithms
  • Degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or Electrical Engineering

Skills that will help you in the role:

  • Proven experience in working in the Investment banking, working with directly with Traders and Desk Heads
  • Advanced expertise in computer networks, computer architecture, and operating systems
  • Agile software development methodology
  • Good scripting skills using Python, Perl or similar
