Macquarie - Securities Lending Trader (Analyst/Senior Analyst)


2023-02-14 - - Hong Kong

This is an exciting opportunity to join our Equity Finance business in a junior Securities Lending trading role. As part of this successful and highly supportive team, you’ll help drive sustained business growth and increased profitability.

On a daily basis, you will be working to originate, identify and execute on profitable trading opportunities.  You will also be responsible for the management and optimisation of equity inventory, with a focus on efficient allocation, rate management, funding, optimizing capital usage and relationship management.

Effective risk and control management is crucial; working to ensure trading risks are foreseen, managed and escalated within the group framework is a vital part of role.  You’ll find yourself partnering with sales and other trading businesses to distribute products aligned to Macquarie’s competitive advantages.

A talented and passionate individual, you will have had exposure to trading either through a front office or related support role, such as Securities Lending middle office.  Exposure to Securities Lending is favourable but not required.  Having strong numerical skills and a high level of proficiency in Excel is a pre-requisite. You’ll enjoy working in a fast-paced environment and you’ll be confident in making sound judgement calls when evaluating risk and reward.

Finally, you will be a confident communicator – as you’ll need to quickly build effective relationships with external clients and counterparties together with internal stakeholders.

You will initially be focused on the Securities Lending product across seven regional markets but if successful, will have the opportunity to grow your knowledge and experience in other areas.  You will be given the freedom to explore new ideas and originate trades, so will need to be self-starting and highly motivated.
