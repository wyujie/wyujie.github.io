About the Position

We are looking to hire a Director of Corporate Security to help ensure the safety and security of our physical spaces within the APAC region. This position sits within our Physical Security team and our larger Technology group. You will be the first security specialist to join our Hong Kong office. This role is an opportunity to join and lead a growing team which supports our global physical security operations to keep our office buildings, disaster recovery sites, and colocation sites protected.

As the Director of Corporate Security, you will take a hands-on approach to complete a wide range of security-related tasks each day. You will ensure the ongoing monitoring and attention to possible disruptive incidents across the APAC region and serve as the primary contact for security intel with the rest of our global team. You will also be responsible for identifying opportunities for strategic improvement to mitigate physical risks and potential vulnerabilities across our public spaces. 

Other responsibilities of the role include:

  • Working closely with Jane Street Security, Facilities, Human Resources, Events, Recruiting and IT teams in all matters involving the safety and security of Streeters, guests and visitors to our locations throughout the APAC region
  • Conducting physical security assessments and audits as necessary
  • Coordinating emergency training, response and evacuation procedures with building management and various Jane Street teams
  • Evaluating changes to the regional security environment and escalating concerns internally to appropriate Jane Street stakeholders
  • Supporting internal security-related investigations as directed
  • Establishing and maintaining key relationships with industry peers, building management teams, local emergency agencies and other public and private organizations 
  • Overseeing the management of contracted security support throughout the region
  • Managing our Regional Security Operations Center’s staffing, training and security response
  • Assisting with the support of our global travel security program

As our business continues to grow, our security challenges evolve and become substantially more complex. Our growing team will benefit from your leadership, expertise and analytical and creative problem-solving skills as we continue to develop and maintain systems that meet the highest standard of security.

About You

  • 10+ years of relevant security experience in the APAC region 
  • Experience in security design, access control and video management technologies; prior experience in crisis management, tabletop exercises or business continuity a plus
  • Motivated self-starter who can handle competing priorities; comfortable making an immediate impact 
  • Is a natural team leader and collaborate with colleagues across several departments and geographic region 
  • Prior financial services industry experience is preferred
  • Fluency in English required; additional regional language ability helpful
