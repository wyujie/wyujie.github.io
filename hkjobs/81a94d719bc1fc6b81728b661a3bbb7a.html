Your key responsibilities:   

  • Executing trades on behalf of the funds as per Portfolio Manager (PMs) instructions
  • Execution in both Equities and Fixed Income
  • Understand our investment strategy, our views and context of the trades
  • Maintain and improve broker relationships
  • Provide market intelligence and flow information to add value
  • Participate in and contribute to internal investment meetings as well as external strategy/analyst meetings
  • Individual must have good communication skills as an important part of the role involves trade/market context dialogue with both PMs and counterparties.

Your skills and experience:

  • Prefer candidate with 8-10years market experience in Equities or Fixed Income in trading/sales/structuring role (Buy-Side or Sell-side)
  • Strong background in APAC markets
  • Proficient in the trading protocols both cash & derivatives
  • Good working knowledge of trading platforms such as Bloomberg, Trade Web, MarketAxess
  • Good working knowledge of Excel
  • Fast learner and can perform under pressure
  • Passionate about market and multi-asset.
