About the role

Fixed Income Credit Strategy is a team in the Private Bank focus on providing leading investment advice in Fixed Income across sub-asset classes for Global Wealth Management Clients. The team has over 80 years of combined credit research experience, with a deep expertise in Rates, Investment Grade, High Yield, EM, Bank Capital and Distressed.

Responsibilities of the team include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Brokerage Recommendation: Provides clients with model lists of our recommended Fixed Income holdings. Managed from a portfolio perspective, these lists are positioned to capitalize on thematic ideas as well as perform through ever-changing market environments, taking into account individual credit fundamental’s as well as market dynamics
  • Extensive portfolio reviews: Reviews of current positioning, identifying credit concerns, opportunities to take profits and appropriate buy replacements
  • The team is constantly scoping the credit landscape for ideas, modelling financials and developing suitable recommendations for clients

Job Responsibilities & Activities:

  • Broad understanding of macro environment and economic trends.
  • Analyze credit markets fundamentals and come up with single name investment ideas.
  • Provide investment recommendations across Global Fixed Income Credit markets with special focus on Asia Credits.
  • Perform client portfolio reviews, serve as Fixed Income Specialist in meetings with clients and advisors.
  • Help drive technology enhancements to day to day operations and improve overall productivity of the business.
  • Track and monitor performance indicators, evaluating the quality of ideas.
  • Model return profile of all the investment ideas generated, and keep track of index returns, total return and alpha.

Skills Required:

  • University degree holder with 5+ years of professional experience in a similar capacity
  • Written and spoken fluency in English required with Mandarin a definite plus
  • Knowledge of economics, financial markets, accounting and corporate finance.
  • Knowledge of fixed income securities and capital structure.
  • Knowledge of financial modeling, specifically having proficiency of Accounting and Corporate Finance to develop a skillset, including analysis of company’s debt profile, cash flows and overall credit risk.
  • Knowledge in Probabilities, Statistics and/or decision sciences.
  • Demonstrated strong presentation skills by delivering technical focused investments presentations and sales driven market strategy presentations.
  • Knowledge in credit analysis in bank capital/financial institution is a plus
  • Data compilation and analysis using MS Excel, Programming language is a plus.
