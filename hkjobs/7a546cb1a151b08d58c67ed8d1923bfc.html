Morgan Stanley - Asia Pacific Finance Project Manager


2023-02-14 - Accounting/Finance - Hong Kong, Hong Kong, Hong Kong

Description


Morgan Stanley is a leading global financial services firm providing a wide range of investment banking, institutional securities, investment management and wealth management services. With offices in more than 42 countries, the Firm's employees serve clients worldwide including corporations, governments, institutions and individuals.


The Finance Division reports to the Chief Financial Officer and has over 3,000 employees worldwide. Finance protects the Morgan Stanley franchise by serving as guardian of the Firm's books and records, and by contributing to firm wide risk management and risk reduction. This division maintains relationships with Morgan Stanley's various industry and government regulators, and also serves as the conduit of financial information to the outside investment community. Finance plays a critical role as advisor to Morgan Stanley's various businesses and its senior management team.


The Division is seeking a Finance Project Manager (PM) / Business Analyst (BA) to be based in the firm's regional headquarters in Hong Kong. This role will provide the individual an opportunity to work within the Asia Finance Organization to deliver business expansion projects and other top risk and regulatory initiatives across multiple markets and asset classes. This opportunity will provide the individual with a chance to work with senior members of the firm in order to drive key business initiatives and collaborate across multiple stakeholders and locations.



Key Roles and Responsibilities


- The Finance Project Manager role focuses on providing project management to support business initiatives, supporting various finance groups such as legal entity control, regulatory reporting, product and cost control, tax, treasury and other finance governance teams.
- Work closely with the Asia Pacific Chief Financial Officer and senior finance managers to oversee and track the portfolio of finance deliverables for business and firm initiatives.
- Interaction with business, technology silos, operations and risk management groups to coordinate front to back testing.
- Assist with managing the pipeline of work for finance organization and facilitating prioritization in line with current supply/demand
- Support standardization and consistency in projects, financial risk management processes and procedures, and ensure adherence to company tools and applications


Required Skills and Experience

- University degree with at least 8+ years of relevant work experience in a major management consulting firm or within the financial services industry
- Knowledge of the financial services industry; preferably in an investment bank with finance organization knowledge
- Working knowledge of financial products, acquired in a risk or business support function
- Strong Project Management skills and demonstrated ability to work under pressure
- Strong analytic and problem solving / process improvement skills, ability to learn quickly and solve poorly defined problems with attention to details
- Excellent verbal and written skills with an ability to create high quality written products that are comprehensive, accurate and tailored to the audience. Fluency in Chinese (Mainland) both spoken and written is essential.
- Outstanding ability to work effectively across all levels of the organization with excellent influencing and negotiation skills
- Strong organizational skills with an ability to manage multiple demands and changing priorities
- Overall experience in hands-on project management, specifically in business-related projects essential and supporting finance organization's processes
- Solid understanding of the Project Lifecycle (PLC) and System Development Lifecycle (SDLC) components.
- MS-Project, MS Excel, and general MS-Office skills essential


Desirable Skills

- Strong knowledge of Asian markets, exchanges, and related financial and regulatory reporting practices in the Asia region. Knowledge of China market is an advantage.
- Experience with large scale regulatory initiatives gained from having worked with the capital markets trading industry (derivatives and equities) at a global bank or consultancy firm
