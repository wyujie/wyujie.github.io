Your future team:

The team mainly manages key strategic initiatives impacting the International Wealth Management business, focused on defining strategy, solutions and plans to manage strategy initiatives in a controlled framework and governance impacting the Business.  Our Initiatives are mostly business driven, focusing on business improvement, growth & expansion, innovation and transformation. The team works cross-functionally with product specialists, client advisors, legal, risk management, compliance, controls, operations, technology and marketing and acts an internal consultant to implement and execute large strategic projects. The team is highly visible and we are seeking top performers who can engage quickly and deliver meaningful impact in a fast-paced environment.
