Position Overview

Details of the Division and Team:

The Chief Risk Office function has Group-wide responsibility for the management and control of all credit, market, operational, enterprise and liquidity risks and has the responsibility of continual development of methods for risk measurement, frameworks and creating a bank wide strong risk culture. Deutsche Bank has been named ‘Bank Risk Manager of the Year’ by the leading publication risk.net for the second consecutive year (2020 & 2021).

Market Risk Management (MRM) is part of our independent Risk function and sits within the Market and Valuations Risk Management group. One of the primary objectives of Market Risk Management is to ensure that our business units’ risk exposure is within the approved risk appetite commensurate with its defined strategy. To achieve this objective, Market Risk Management works closely together with risk takers (“the business units”) and other control and support groups.

Your key responsibilities:

The successful applicant will be at Vice President level and will be the Head of the Market Risk team in Hong Kong. The team oversees all market risk matters for all the businesses in Hong Kong including the Investment Bank (FX, Rates, Credit Trading and Financing. Equity Capital Market and Equity Financing), Corporate Bank, and Wealth Management.

Key responsibilities include

  • Oversee and manage all market risk taken by the Business in Hong Kong, and act as a point of contact for key internal and external stakeholders (business, infrastructure functions, auditors and regulators).

  • Implement and maintain a comprehensive framework to independently identify, measure, monitor and manage market risk, across all positions, regardless of their classification in the trading or banking book.

  • Contribute to the process for setting risk appetite for market risk and ensure it is mapped to the bank strategy and capital planning.

  • Allocate defined appetite for Hong Kong businesses by establishing limits, monitoring their usage, investigating and escalating any breaches in accordance to the internal escalation process.

  • Constantly assess that the market risk measurement processes are appropriate in terms of both completeness and capturing the nature of the risks within the portfolio.

  • Design and perform stress testing scenarios.

  • Review and approve new products and transactions, especially in the Equity Capital Market and Equity Financing space.

  • Proactively lead market risk initiatives impacting the region and Hong Kong (IBOR transition, FRTB implementation, intraday risk management, audit remediation, etc...)

  • Escort, mentor and develop the MRM regional teams.

Your skills and experience:

  • Minimum bachelor’s degree, preferably in a quantitative discipline. CFA/FRM a plus.

  • Established experience (>10 years) in market risk management with particular focus on Asian Market. Previous experience in trading will be highly regarded.

  • A strong understanding of markets, products across multiple asset classes (Fixed Income, Credit, FX, Equity), financial crisis, derivative products pricing and risk, local regulations, and market risk.

  • A strong candidate who will be able to challenge, while at the same time being able to build relationships with both senior Front Office traders and risk staff.

  • An experienced manager who will be able to mentor and develop the more junior members of the team and set the goal for the team.

  • A problem solver who is able to work independently and under pressure on a trading floor.

  • Good communication skills and the ability to build constructive relationships with business and other infrastructure teams. Fluent English is highly preferred.
