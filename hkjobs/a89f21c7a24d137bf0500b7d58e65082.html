Under the central control management function, the team is responsible for international reporting, international program execution and governance.

The team is a firm-wide Reporting Utility that provides standardized control-related data and drives efficient and accurate reporting. It is an information warehouse with capabilities for reporting, visualizations and analytics. Its primary goal is to enhance control oversight and efficiency through the standardization and automation of operational risk reporting, provide access to firmwide aggregated information and produce business risk insight.

Supporting locations outside of North America, the team also provides oversight and governance support on the implementation of operational risk and control framework across international locations and cross line of businesses as well as the execution of key Controls programs. 

Promoting global governance and reporting consistency & standardization, the job holder will provide stakeholder support to the Regional hubs across Asia Pacific (APAC), Europe Middle East & Africa (EMEA) & Latin America (LATAM).  The job holder will have a direct reporting line into the Governance Lead, International Reporting Central team in Hong Kong.

 

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

Governance

  • Drive consistence in operational risk and control framework governance across international locations and cross line of businesses
  • Provide advisory support on operational risk and control framework implementation
  • Support regional senior management in better monitoring of the Firm’s risk and control environment through identification, analysis and escalation of key control issues and enterprise risk themes
  • Drive training and communications for firmwide control programs across lines of business and international locations

Reporting

  • Compile, produce and manage data, analytics and reporting for multiple audiences, metrics repository, issue management reporting and materials for cross regional governance committees and forums
  • Perform Quality Assurance, testing and control of data, analytics and reporting before delivery to ensure consistency and accuracy cross regionally
  • Support the development of strategic reporting requirements with particular focus on report automation and standardization
  • Manage and cultivate cross functional working relationships with regional business partners throughout the firm including Regional Control Managers, Business and Functional Control Managers, Operational Risk Managers & Compliance Officers

 

Program Execution

  • Facilitate the delivery on key Controls projects, programs and work-streams across international locations, driving reporting enhancements, data acquisition & data discovery activities whilst ensuring regional requirements are embedded where appropriate
  • Support and monitor the status and development of global and regional projects for project sponsors and respective stakeholders with issue/dependencies escalation as and when appropriate
  • Work with IT function to ensure reporting defects and enhancements are appropriately managed and addressed for ongoing reporting needs
