UBS - Software Engineer

Your role

Do you believe in collaboration between business- and technical experts as a key success factor to achieve best results? • experience in common development tooling and familiar with CI/CD processes (Jenkins, Maven, GitLab, Sonar etc.) and approaches; experience to implement automation to streamline development pipeline
• experience with agile methodologies, especially scrum, iterative development and continuous release approaches
• proven ability to produce secure, stable and good performing code and write optimized, clean code
• ability to lead investigation of technical issues, both in a highly secured production environment as well as lower test environment
• experience working in a highly complex and integrated banking platform, ability to traverse and communicate with different teams to negotiate interface design as well as troubleshoot integration issues
• experience and knowledge in cloud native development (especially Azure)
