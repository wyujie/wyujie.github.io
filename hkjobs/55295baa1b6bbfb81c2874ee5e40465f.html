Market Connectivity Developer - Trading Desk / Broker

We are looking for a candidate with financial markets technology experience to work within the Market Connectivity team. The Market Connectivity team is a global team, with the primary responsibility of developing front office trading solutions with brokers, exchanges, ECN’s and third party platforms across all asset classes and regions.

Principal Responsibilities

The candidate will be responsible for developing software applications supporting electronic trading with exchanges, brokers and ECNs with an immediate focus on equities, futures and options. Additional development responsibilities may include monitoring, research and statistical analysis and automation projects.

Qualifications/Skills Required

  • 5+ years Java programming experience, C++ experience is a plus.

  • A solid understanding of object oriented and low latency programming is required.

  • 3+ years of financial institution experience required.

  • FIX protocol knowledge is required, FIX 4.2 – FIX 5.0

  • Knowledge of binary trading protocols is a plus.

  • Extensive Linux development experience required.

  • Must have a strong understanding of trading equities, futures and FX products.

  • Must have basic networking/connectivity skills – i.e. TCP/IP & multicast.

  • Must be able to troubleshoot production issues (Level "3" support).

  • Must have excellent written and verbal communications skills.
