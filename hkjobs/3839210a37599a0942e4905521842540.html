HSBC - Controls Lead - Wealth and Personal Banking


2023-02-11 - Technology - Kowloon City, Hong Kong

Job description

Some careers have more impact than others.

Wealth and Personal Banking is our new global business combining Retail Banking and Wealth Management; and Global Private Banking, to become one of the world’s largest global wealth managers with USD1.4 trillion in assets. Across Asia, where wealth pools are growing faster than in any other region, HSBC’s wealth revenues grew 12% in 2019 (year-on-year) to USD5.7 billion.  Our dedicated colleagues serve millions of customers worldwide across the entire spectrum of private wealth, ranging from personal banking individuals and families, through to business owners, investors and ultra-high-net-worth individuals.  We provide products and services such as bank accounts, credit cards, personal loans and mortgages, as well as asset management, insurance, wealth management and private banking, that best suit our customers’ needs.

We are currently seeking a high calibre professional to join our team as a Controls Lead .

Principal Responsibilities

  • Direct and manage detailed analysis to support automated control compliance across open source acquisition and software delivery and deployment and security controls
  • Have accountability for the delivery of assurance and controls reviews across Cloud workloads, and Cloud platforms
  • Work across analysis and development teams to drive analysis tasks and implementation of controls automation
  • Be future focused and be able to take a global view from both a Customer and Business perspective
  • Resolve issues and support discussions to drive a successful outcome at a detailed level where needed
  • Lead and co-ordinate the production of material evidencing controls position for Cloud platforms, to be presented at Governance forums to management, stakeholders, and customers
  • Listen to and engage teams and stakeholders to solicit feedback on controls compliance and process improvements
Requirements

Requirements

  • Demonstrable expert knowledge in operational risk management, internal control, or internal audit preferably within a large financial institution and / or IT risk consultancy
  • Experienced risk management leader with proven experience working with public cloud services, platforms and technologies across one or more of AliCloud, Amazon Web Services, Google Cloud Platform, MS Azure
  • Cross border Payments and FX knowledge will be an asset
  • A university graduate with degree in IT, finance or a related discipline
  • Business and technical knowledge on banking system would be an advantage
  • Able to work independently, under pressure and proactively
  • Strong interpersonal, communication and presentation skills
  • Proficient in spoken and written English
