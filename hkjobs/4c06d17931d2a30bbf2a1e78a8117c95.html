Role Description 

This role is responsible for leading the delivery of core product enhancements and targeted tactical and strategic solutions to meet the extensive needs of the clients and prospects for the Custody business. Our aim is to position J.P. Morgan as ‘best in class’ with clients and prospects. The Product Development Team also maintains and expands subject matter expertise, providing thought leadership in the Custody product space. 

 Based in Hong Kong, the Custody Product Development Manager will be responsible for delivering the Custody Investment Initiative programmes and implementing Product solutions and technology changes aligned to the strategic roadmap for the Custody business.

This is an exciting opportunity to work in a complex, challenging and changing environment at one of the world’s leading custodians.

 

Responsibilities

  • Delivery of strategic Custody Investment Initiatives and the Custody roadmap
  • Planning initiative delivery timeframes, refining budgetary requirements and ensuring completion of projects and key milestones within agreed budget and timeframes
  • Establish governance framework to ensure stakeholders, sponsors and impacted parties receive relevant and timely communication within required standards.
  • Working with functional partners including Product Management, Technology, Client Services, Network Management and other Operational areas to deliver agreed solutions
  • Planning and coordinate test phases as required, ensuring success criteria clearly defined and clearly met at test conclusion
  • Striving for robust strategic solutions, encourage others to think strategically and leverage full intellectual capability of subject matter experts and programme team
  • Presentation of project objectives, progress and benefits to varied audiences including senior management, J.P. Morgan clients and functional partners

 

Your Background

The successful candidate will ideally have worked in Securities Services or Treasury Services environment for a minimum of 10 years in an Operations, Client Service Product Management or Product Development.

Key attributes include:

  • Results orientated: Drive results through people, communication, influence management and interactions. Demonstrates a proven track record of delivering strategic projects
  • Analytical: strong analytical skills with complementary communications and presentation skills
  • Multi-tasker: Ability to multi-task, effectively prioritise workloads, and manage multiple streams of work concurrently
  • Ownership: be personally accountable for achieving key milestones on a development roadmap
  • Strong influencing and negotiation skills
  • Communication: Confident communicator in all mediums across varied audiences combined with the ability to interact with senior executives
  • Initiative: be able to move initiatives forward in a cross-functional, multi-locational and matrixed organizational model
  • Decision making: Ability to make considered decisions based upon fact and business / industry knowledge
  • Integrity: High levels of integrity, operating on the basis of open and honest interaction
  • Teammate: be a strong team player and an ability to work in teams distributed across the globe
