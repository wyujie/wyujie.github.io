Market Data Engineer

We are looking for a strong candidate with financial markets technology experience to support our co-location based low latency environment in the APAC region. The successful candidate must be comfortable speaking directly with Portfolio managers, traders , IT groups and external parties. Competency in market data and networking, with a focus on direct feeds, is a requirement.

Primary Responsibility:

  • The support of the low latency environment, including overseeing changes, monitoring, assisting with new builds and working with Trading groups, IT and external vendors.
  • Ensuring system stability, whilst successfully managing change.
  • Monitoring the low latency environment, including enhancing and developing monitoring systems as required.
  • Promote operational efficiency through driving automation, monitoring and documentation.

Skills Required:

  • Ability to curate and maintain excellent working relationships with traders, other IT staff and vendors.
  • Knowledge of network protocols and operating systems in a low latency environment
  • Familiarity with Asian trading venue market data feeds and protocols.
  • Experience with maintaining market data feedhandlers.
  • Coding or scripting knowledge/experience. Able to develop toolsets to support the environment.
  • The ability to manage and prioritise multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment
  • Contributing towards the team’s technical direction by driving new initiatives

Desirable skills:

  • Experience in infrastructure performance tuning.
  • Familiarity with basic Reuters, Bloomberg, Exchange symbologies and data models.
  • Any software development experience in C++.
  • Any SRE (Site Reliability Engineering) experience.
