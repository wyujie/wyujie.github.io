Overview:

DWS Group (DWS) is one of the world’s leading asset managers with EUR 821bn of assets under management (as of 31 December 2022). Building on more than 60 years of experience, it has a reputation for excellence in Germany, Europe, the Americas and Asia. DWS is recognized by clients globally as a trusted source for integrated investment solutions, stability, and innovation across a full spectrum of investment disciplines.

The Alternatives Specialists team sits within Client Coverage Division (CCD) of DWS and is a global team based in 12 offices across 9 countries. The APAC Alternatives Specialist team works closely in partnership with the APAC Generalist Sales teams to deliver against the business plans for illiquid alternatives strategy/product sales in the region.

Alternatives Specialist is the dedicated sales distribution and investor relations group within the CCD for the illiquid alternatives asset class offerings structured and launched by the Product Division and managed by the Investment Division. The illiquid alternatives asset class offerings include Real Estate, Infrastructure, Private Equity, Private Debt and Sustainable Investments. The group works closely with the sales generalists and other specialized teams, such as Insurance Advisory, Private Bank Coverage, Client Relations and Retail) within CCD to ensure the delivery of quality solutions to our clients who want to invest into illiquid alternatives.
