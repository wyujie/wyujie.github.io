The Team:

The Asset Management Derivatives team (AMD) is a global center of excellence for derivatives portfolio management. The team operates in partnership with the whole of Asset Management in delivering derivative mandates to our clients both internal and external.  This includes over $40bn of derivative overlay portfolios managed by the team. 

AMD was formed in response to the growth in demand for derivative solutions across the asset management landscape. The group was formed to provide more sophisticated investment strategies in response to a range of catalysts including the low yield environment, the economic cycle, and desire for diversification away from traditional assets. The team has a wide remit to respond to internal and external client requests for derivative strategies including overlays. AMD provides a multi-asset global centre of derivative expertise that drives innovation in the derivative solutions space and partners with our traditional lines of business to leverage existing investment capabilities. The team brings with it a wealth of experience from both the buy and sell-side with extensive experience in equity, rates, currency, credit and volatility derivatives.

The Role:

The Asset Management Derivatives is currently looking for a Portfolio Manager to help grow the APAC team in Hong Kong. The team has a global footprint with portfolio managers in New York, London and Hong Kong. As a relatively new team this is an opportunity to help shape the strategy of the team as well as gain exposure to the current mandates.
