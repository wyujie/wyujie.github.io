Barclays - RAD Pricing Support Developer


2023-02-12 - Technology - Hong Kong, Hong Kong China

Front Office IT RAD Developer
Hong Kong

As Barclays Front Office IT RAD Developer, you will be involved in the design, maintenance and enhancement of applications / tools from 3 main streams: Pricing, Risk and Data Analytics stream in delivering support to the equity desk traders. The Front Office IT team currently owns a set of tools to enable the trading desk to do ad-hoc pricing and analysis on top of the risk results produced by the risk systems and market data. You will also need to interact with Structurers, Risk Management, Operations, and Finance clients as well as global team members.

Barclays is one of the world's largest and most respected financial institutions, with 329 years of success, quality and innovation behind us. We offer careers that provide endless opportunity – helping millions of individuals and businesses thrive, and creating financial and digital solutions that the world now takes for granted.

What will you be doing?

  • Designing and implementing of front-office application from front to back including computing grid pricing & configuration, database design, standalone pricer, web API etc
  • Gathering user requirements & implement different tools / reports ranging from General RiskViewer for displaying all kinds of greeks in different scale and aggregation etc
  • Being innovative and driving key architectural decisions to come up something new and deliver the FOIT Strategy
  • You will interact with different people, Trading/Operations and Finance clients as well as work closely with different region team members
  • Being involved in the integration of FO IT solutions into other framework where required

What we’re looking for:

  • Have Bachelor’s degree or foreign equivalent in Mathematics, Financial Engineering, Computer Science, or related field
  • Have five plus years of hands on development experience with one or more programming languages, not limited to C# / Python / VBA etc.
  • Have hands on development experience with databases design, SQL, Bloomberg API and BI tools such as Tableau.
  • Be proficient in software development process, knowledge of data structures, algorithms & general problem solving skills and best practices (TDD, code reviews etc.)

Skills that will help you in the role:

  • Knowledge of Exotic Equities Derivatives financial products and related risk measures and market data
  • Experience on VLDB
  • Experience on Restful / Web API development
  • Experience in automation using scripting languages including JavaScript, VB and/or Batch File Scripting, Autosys.

Where will you be working?

Our offices are situated in the center of Hong Kong’s financial district, at 2 Queen’s Road.

Cheung Kong Centre is an investment banking hub that offers panoramic views of Victoria Harbour and Kowloon Peninsula. There is a variety of local amenities and you will be just a short walk from shopping malls, health clubs and multiple transport links.
