J.P. Morgan - Infrastructure Project Manager, Vice President


2023-01-24 - Project Execution - Hong Kong

The Project Management role is focused on managing the lifecycle of a project end to end including requirements gathering, designs, funding, planning, scheduling, implementation along with tracking progress and risk & issues.

You will be driving the delivery of projects across all services working closely with your project team, across all regions and multiple GTI & LOB technology teams while interfacing with senior managers and stakeholders in the organization. The role requires broad business process experience, relationship building and client management skills.  Projects may be large cross-functional in nature or major strategic initiatives and may be underway or planned. Strong leadership, excellent oral and written communications skills and the ability to remain calm and perform under pressure are a must have. 

Key Responsibilities

  • Responsible for running a variety of infrastructure projects with focus to corporate office build outs, office relocations and desktop technology deployments
  • Ensure all projects are delivered with high quality, on scope, on time & on budget
  • Provide governance and controls to all project financials to include developing business cases, forecasting, understanding P&L, depreciation.
  • Keep all projects on track escalating decisions to executive sponsors as necessary
  • Organizing the design and development of the Project
  • Ensure risk management strategies are followed and reviewed within guidelines
  • Plan and set project goals and milestones based on approved Charter / Statement of Works
  • Identify and secure resources including project team, vendors and consultants
  • Detect and solve project risk/issues effectively
  • Lead project team
  • Ensure project management standards and rigor is applied
  • Develop communications to executive sponsor & project stakeholders; conduct regularly scheduled briefings/status updates
  • Serve as single point of contact for project level risk & issues / questions / escalations
  • Prepares and/or provides project reporting, metrics, scorecards, etc.
  • Partners with key stakeholders to identify project inter-dependencies that may influence project success. 
  • Supports team in distributing project information to executive management, and key stakeholders.
  • Create an environment that breeds trust and collaboration with others.  Exhibit a strategic business focus with a customer-focused perspective.
  • Recognize areas for internal improvement and developing plans for implementing them.
  • Comply with company standards and procedures
  • Budget / Financial Management including Business Case completion
  • Evaluation of impact to Total Cost of Ownership for multiple simultaneous, complex projects
