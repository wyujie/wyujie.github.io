About the Position

We are looking for a HR Generalist to support our growing HR team as we continue to find ways to improve employee experience in our Asia offices. 

This role is for someone who loves helping people and solving problems. You'll work alongside our seasoned team to learn how we create a rewarding employee experience, and the strategy behind our approach. Our ability to get the little things right and the personal attention we give each of our employees is what drives our HR success and our culture. With every task, we balance efficiency with a genuine empathy for our incredible colleagues.

You will be trusted to field a wide range of employee inquiries and advice and connect people with the training and support they need to be successful in their roles. You will also help maintain HR databases with employee files and records. These responsibilities require incredible attention to detail, and the highest levels of internal and external confidentiality. 

You’ll also play a direct role in supporting our employees with queries around our policies and processes, while also assisting in HR projects and operational improvements. These projects will provide an opportunity to collaborate across global teams. We don’t expect you to know everything immediately, but the right person will be eager to learn and not afraid to ask questions. You should be willing to accept feedback from others and look at any mistake as an opportunity to learn and grow.

This is an opportunity to work with a small group of extremely dedicated people who take pride in making our firm an amazing place to work.
