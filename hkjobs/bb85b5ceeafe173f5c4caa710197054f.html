UBS - Market Risk Analyst - Equities


2023-01-28 - Risk - Hong Kong

Your role

The role is for a Market Risk Officer covering APAC Equity trading portfolios within the Global Markets business based in Hong Kong.

Are you interested in a career in risk control, focusing on market risks? Are you curious about the risks arising from Equity Derivative & Financing Products? Do you work well within a team? We’re looking for someone like you to:

• Review daily trading and market activity, resulting risk profile for the identification, analysis and control of the market risk exposures.
• Create transparency for stakeholders through the preparation and provision of meaningful and timely risk information and relevant advice.
• Undertaking portfolio risks; perform risk assessments and approve transactions within the delegated market risk authority and in accordance with internal policies and regulations.
• Working alongside the business, as the 2nd line of defence, to independently identify and escalate issues and concerns.
• Enhancing the risk control framework and participating in corresponding projects in order to accommodate product innovation and regulatory developments.
• Reviewing the appropriateness of limits frameworks and related controls.
• Maintaining a climate of openness, transparency and accountability in the team.

Your team

You’ll be based on the trading floor working in the APAC Equities Market Risk Control team in Hong Kong. The global team is spread across APAC, US and EMEA regions, you will also closely work with colleagues in Australia, Japan, Singapore, London and New York.
We monitor the portfolio risk of relevant trading desks to ensure that risk exposures are appropriately diversified, that the risk profile is in line with our risk appetite, and that non-standard risks are adequately identified, controlled, and made transparent.

Your expertise

You have:
• A university degree in finance, economics, engineering or a numerical discipline.
• Minimum 5 years of experience in Market Risk, Risk Control, Valuation, Finance or Trading
• Strong analytical skills and the ability to apply quantitative techniques to solve practical problems
• A keen interest and understanding of financial markets and equity products.
• Experience in markets-related roles, with an emphasis on market risk management of Equity products. Front Office experience would be of benefit..
• Excellent communication skills with the ability to explain technical topics clearly and intuitively. Willingness to build relationships across businesses and divisions.
• Basic programming and automation skills desired
• A collaborative nature and enjoy the challenge of working in a global, diverse team.
