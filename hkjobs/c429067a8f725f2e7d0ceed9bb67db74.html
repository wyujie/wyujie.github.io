The Re Entry Program is a 15-week fellowship program, beginning March 20, 2023 and ending June 30, 2023, with the prospect of an offer for permanent employment with JP Morgan Chase at the end of the program. The program offers a reintroduction to corporate life for those returning to the workplace after an extended career break for two or more years. The fellowship placements will be based on both business needs and candidate skill set and interest areas within our Corporate Investment Banking, Asset Wealth Management & Commercial Banking Operations business. Please refer to our ReEntry Overview page for further information regarding the Program

Overview  - GFCC

The Global Financial Crimes Compliance (GFCC) Asia team is responsible for the implementation and oversight of the Anti-Money Laundering (AML) policies and procedures for the firm's businesses in Asia. GFCC works closely with the business and other control functions to identify the level of risk associated with the firm's products, services, customers, transactions, and geographic locations, so as to develop appropriate measures to monitor, control, and mitigate money laundering and sanctions related risks.

Key responsibilities:

  • The successful candidate will principally be involved in the day-to-day functions of the AML Compliance team, providing support on financial crimes matters related to the different Lines of Businesses  (“LOB”), implement and maintain relevant AML policies and procedures, as well as performing relevant compliance reviews on financial crime risks.

Qualifications and Skillsets we are looking for:

  • Bachelor’s degree with minimum 3 years related working experience
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills
  • Strong problem solving skills, with strong attention to detail and able to articulate well
  • Pro-active, organized and able to work collaboratively and independently
  • Motivated and conscientious self-starter who appreciates challenges and takes ownership to improve the status quo
  • Able to prioritize multiple tasks, work under pressure and meet tight deadlines
  • Proficient with Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint
