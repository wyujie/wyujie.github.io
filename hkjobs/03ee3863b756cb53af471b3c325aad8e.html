Point 72 - Data Operations Analyst


2023-02-01 - Data Science & Analytics - Hong Kong   |   Singapore

Role

The Cubist data team is looking for an exceptional individual to oversee the daily operation of their automated data systems. 

Job Responsibilities

  • Daily validation of critical data points, in particular relating to corporate actions.
  • Processing of unstructured data and manual data entry.
  • Responding to user requests and assisting with access control to databases.
  • Creating various types of reports and internal audits.
  • Creating and maintaining documentation and wiki pages.

Desirable Candidates

  • Undergraduate or higher degree in a quantitative discipline.
  • Experience with Python programming.
  • Familiarity with SQL databases, database principles and ability to write basic queries.
  • Understanding of dividends and corporate actions.
  • Familiarity with financial data for global Equities.
  • Attention to detail and strong troubleshooting ability
