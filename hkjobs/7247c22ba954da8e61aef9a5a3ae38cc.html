An Internship with Point72’s Operations Team

The Operations team provides support for all operational aspects of trade processing, reconciliation, financing, and reporting for the Firm globally. We help ensure the success of the Firm’s daily trading activities for all products, including equities, derivatives, bonds, foreign exchange, and futures across global markets. Our team works directly with traders and counterparties to resolve trading matters and supports the credit and treasury functions.


We are looking for a bright, articulate, and motivated individual to intern with us next summer. If you want to join a world-class investment management organization that operates in a fast-paced, dynamic environment and rewards talent with recognition and increased responsibility, then this is the right opportunity. In this role, you will gain exposure and knowledge to:


What you’ll do

  • Work directly with traders on trade bookings, trade verification, corporate actions, PnL, and technology and legal related issues
  • Coordinate between portfolio managers, traders, and outside counterparties to resolve issues in a timely fashion
  • Review and process Asian and European voluntary and mandatory corporate actions and assess position implications
  • Set up new securities for various asset types, ensure settlement of all Asian trades, and aid in reconciling Asian FX and Futures trades daily for teams based in the U.S.
  • Monitor and reduce foreign currency exposure that is attributed to PnL, exposure on ADRs, and dollarized swaps 

What’s required

  • You are expected to obtain your bachelor’s degree between December 2023 to July 2024
  • Demonstrated academic success as reflected in a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.3/4.0
  • Prior internship experience
  • Strong time management skills coupled with the ability to efficiently prioritize, work independently, and make sound decisions
  • Ability stay organized, pay attention to details, multitask, and work under pressure
  • Proficiency in Excel
  • A base knowledge of International markets, corporate actions, futures, FX and OTC products a plus
  • Commitment to the highest ethical standards

Deadline for applications is 11:59PM HKT on December 31, 2022.
