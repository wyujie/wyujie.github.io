Point 72 - Business Development, Asia Pacific


2023-01-15 - Talent Acquisition - Hong Kong

A Career with Point72’s Business Development

Business Development (BD) is a dedicated talent acquisition function focused on identifying and attracting Equity Long/Short investment talent at the PM, Senior Analyst, and Analyst level. We conduct thorough due diligence on candidates beginning with an extensive, multi-step interview process through to qualitative and quantitative assessments. Our success is dependent upon talent sourcing and relationship building, and we continue our relationships with new hires to assist with team development and support PMs as they build and execute on a business plan. 



What you’ll do

  • Build regular dialogue and relationships with financial services industry professionals including buy-side, sell-side, allocators and others to provide a key source of talent idea generation in addition to market color 
  • Actively source potential candidates, including utilizing recruitment agencies
  • Work closely with Head of BD, AeJ and Management on the recruitment strategy to attract top talent
  • Responsible for managing the recruitment life-cycle
  • Manage the candidate tracking system to track applicants
  • Develop strong relationships with recruitment agencies including fee negotiation and managing vendor performance
  • Partner with Compliance internally to understand and manage compliance risks

What’s required

We’re looking for an experienced recruiter with meaningful exposure to institutional investors, who is extremely motivated, proactive, and solution oriented. Specifically, you will need:

  • 3 years of relevant experience, specifically as an investment professional recruiter, cap intro, PB or equity sales in the L/S equity strategies space
  • Demonstrated comfort level in a similar role, which is front line and client facing in nature
  • Ability to think strategically and develop long-range business plans that will support the organization’s strategic vision
  • Proven ability to successfully execute against multiple and ambitious business initiatives with thoughtful and timely results
  • Strong negotiating skills
  • A history of building and leveraging relationships and a reputation for collaboration
  • Strong organizational acumen
  • Substantial educational background with an undergraduate degree
  • Bilingual in English and Mandarin
  • Commitment to the highest ethical standards

We take care of our people

We invest in our people, their careers, their health, and their well-being. When you work here, we provide:

  • Health care benefits
  • Generous parental and family leave policies
  • Mental and physical wellness programs
  • Volunteer opportunities
  • Non-profit matching gift program
  • Support for employee-led affinity groups representing women, minorities and the LGBT+ community
  • Tuition assistance
  • Competitive retirement program
