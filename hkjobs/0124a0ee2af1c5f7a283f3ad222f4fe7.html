J.P. Morgan - Wealth Management, Night Desk Trader - Associate/Analyst


2023-02-14 - Client Advisory - Central and Western, Hong Kong Island, Hong Kong

J.P. Morgan Private Bank Asia is looking for a diligent and professional Trader to join the Night Desk team. Coverage of US Equity, Derivatives, Fixed Income and FX is the main focus. This is a permanent role based in Hong Kong.

Instead of using the reactive model (order taking and execution), J.P. Morgan offers a more advanced approach, the Night Desk Trader provides quality services by contacting clients on their interest investment area and market news, as well as sourcing the best execution for clients.  

Working Hours will be 6:30pm until 4am; Monday to Friday.

Great career progression will be offered to the right individual who has experience in a single asset class trading but would like to widen investment knowledge and progress to Private Banking client facing role either as a Banker, Investor or Product Specialist.

As an integral part of the integrated team, the Night Desk Trader will have opportunity to attend the office at different shifts, for example to meet clients or attend the team meetings.

Qualifications

  • Relevant trading desk experience is required
  • Solid product knowledge and trading experience in US Equity and/or FX
  • Fluency in English required with Mandarin and/or Cantonese a definite plus
  • Good understanding of US and Asia markets
  • Good knowledge of Bloomberg system
  • Good client facing, communication and interpersonal skills
