J.P. Morgan - 2023 Winning Women Program - Hong Kong


2023-02-14 - Analysts - Hong Kong

Winning Women

Building a diverse team is a critical part of our success. At J.P. Morgan, we welcome candidates from all backgrounds and perspectives to join us as we grow and develop our businesses for the future. You will join a collaborative, inclusive and supportive community where your perspective and work contribution is critical to the way we conduct business and deliver solutions for our clients. Be a part of one of the world’s largest financial institutions; work on global financial projects that have the ability to impact communities, and design innovative and sustainable suite of financial solutions for our clients that spans across 100 countries.


What Can I Learn?  

The Winning Women Early Insight program aims to empower women and those who seeks to embark on a career in financial services. Through fireside chats and sharing sessions, you’ll meet with our female business leaders who will share with you their personal stories and career journey at J.P. Morgan. Be inspired by their experiences, and draw on some of the most important career tips and advice to help you understand your strengths and passion. Depending on your interest and skills set, you will be assigned to specific business tracks where you can gain a deeper understanding towards that area of business, and the breadth of career opportunities that you can find at J.P. Morgan.

The Winning Women program also features a series of professional development workshops, where participants can gain real-world, practical soft skills from subject-matter experts. Topics covered at these workshops personal branding, elevator pitch, effective public speaking/presentation skills, and negotiation skills.

 

When is Winning Women?

Join us for a series of workshops and interactive sessions between March 27 to April 21, 2023. Participants can attend sessions based on their interest and skills sets indicated in the application. Sessions are typically held in the evening, between 7:00pm to 9:00pm HKT/SGT, and will be hosted virtually through Zoom.


Who Are We Inviting?  
If you're highly motivated and enjoy working in diverse teams to drive innovative business solutions - this program is for you!  Applicants of all genders and various academic disciplines are welcome to apply!

  • Attentive to detail
  • Eager to learn and develop
  • Strong collaboration skills
  • Ability to think critically and analyze data
  • Basic understanding of financial services
  • Strong verbal and written communication
  • Fluency in English
  • Pursuing a degree qualification from any academic background
  • Expected graduation date of November 2024 – July 2025

 

How Do I Register?

  1. Complete the online application on J.P. Morgan’s website.
  2. Indicate the business track(s) you’re interested in on your online application. Business tracks available for this event include:
  • Asset Management, Corporate Banking, Compliance, Finance & Business Management, Human Resources, Investment Banking, Markets, Operations, Technology, Wealth Management, and Wholesale Payments.
  1. If you are advanced to the next step of the registration process, you’ll receive an email invitation to complete a short video interview, powered by HireVue.
  2. Selected candidates will be notified via email in mid-March 2023 with further program details.
  3. The registration deadline (to complete both the online application and HireVue) is Wednesday, March 16, 2023 (23:59 HKT/SGT). You’re strongly encouraged to apply early as application screening will be conducted on a rolling basis.

** Please note this is an invite only event - selected attendees will receive the event details through a confirmation email prior to the event.
