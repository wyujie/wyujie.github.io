Your key responsibilities:

  • Responsible for trading of Day shift, whereby covering global markets during Asian hours. Experiences in executions of equities and additional experiences of other asset class (fixed income/structured product) would be a plus.

  • Provide timely and accurate dealing and booking after trades. Communicate effectively with internal & external parties on order status.

  • Hands-on experiences in trading systems (ie. Bloomberg EMSX, Reuters ROMS). Also, familiarities with algorithmic trading strategies via Bloomberg and Reuters.

  • Team player; possess with solid experience in dealing and can work independently to deliver KPI without intensive supervisions required. Responsible to ensure all activities are in adherence as per Compliance & Risk.

  • Language proficiency in Cantonese, Mandarin and English

Your skills and experience:

  • Bachelor or above Degree holder, preferably in Finance, Economics or related discipline.

  • At least 2 years relevant trading experience, covering global markets.

  • Can work independently to trade global markets during Asian hours. Knowledge in capital markets, especially in equities, fixed income and structured products.

  • Language proficiencies in Mandarin, Cantonese, English.

  • Self-motivated and passionate about Private Banking business
