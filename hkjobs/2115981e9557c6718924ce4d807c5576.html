UBS - Data & Document Specialist


2023-02-02 - Operations - Hong Kong

Your role

Are you incredibly organized with an eye for detail? Do you know how to take care of business? We’re looking for someone like that to:
• review and process incoming documents for clients
• provide client advisors support with document management
• ensure that the document records correspond with data in the system
• offer advice and support to client advisors when carrying out administrative tasks and managing archive systems

Your team

You’ll be working in the Upfront Documents Centre team in Hong Kong. You will be responsible for converting documents (external and internal) to digital format using high-resolution scanner. You will primarily handle documents from reception to archival – this includes steps like pre-scan checks, scanning, data capturing, rejection handling and archival as well as retrieval of archived, stored documents. Work involves standard office setting, with the ability to handle time sensitive documents and operate office equipment. In addition, staff will need to support in the partnership with Change the Bank team (CtB) to perform User Acceptance Testing (UAT) for changes relating to job scope.

Your expertise

• a university degree
• the right mindset for change to drive process improvement and adapt to changing conditions
• working knowledge of MS word, excel, simple indexing and web-based applications
• meticulous and detail oriented, comfortable with repetitive tasks
• able to manage multiple, high priority tasks and adjust to shifting priorities, while meeting deadlines
• able to communicate clearly and concisely, both verbal and in writing
• client service oriented and enjoys dealing with people (enjoy working in a team based environment, adaptable interpersonal skills)
• able to work independently with minimal direction and maintain production area in a neat and orderly condition
